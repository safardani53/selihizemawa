Playboy Twins Strike 7-Figure Porn Deal

Though Hugh Hefner died in 2017, his legacy endures — and so do many of the women who became famous through Playboy.

Karissa and Kristina Shannon rose to fame as twin models — who dated Hugh Hefner at the same time.

Now, the two of them have secured a whopping porn deal that’s going to take their career to the next level.

Karissa Shannon and Kristina Shannon are poised to make bank on their greatest asset: themselves.

The pair of them tells The Blast that they have signed a seven-figure deal with Brazzers.

Brazzers is a porn company based in Canada that has won numerous over the years for its adult streaming content.

Karissa and Kristina were approached by Brazzers but took their time carefully considering the offer.

After they decided to go through with it, there were some extensive negotiations.

Ultimately, the twins accepted.

Shooting dates, The Blast has been informed, have already been set.

Karissa and Kristina will share some scenes, while of course abiding by industry rules and all relevant laws.

(Meaning that they’ll be on camera at the same time but, for extremely obvious reasons, the sisters will not interact with each other sexually)

Both Karissa and Kristina will film boy/girl scenes, girl/girl scenes, boy/girl/girl scenes, and at least one girl group scene.

Though no strangers to baring all for the camera, this will be a new and exciting direction for the twins’ careers.

The Shannon twins spoke directly to The Blast.

Among other things, they believe that Hugh Hefner would have been proud to see his exes take this deal.

“Hef would be proud that we are reinventing ourselves,” they shared.

They noted: “just like he had done throughout his legacy!”

“There’s no doubt we are the closest twins on the planet!” they added.

There’s a tagline, for you.

The Shannon twins are pumped “to finally share with everyone that we are complete f–king monsters in the bedroom.”

They will be filming “exclusively with the best … Brazzers!”

Now, this new career isn’t without certain new, somewhat odd considerations.

Both women say that they are in the process of getting their butts insured … for $1 million.

We know that the claim sounds like a joke, but if your livelihood is based upon your bodypart … you could lose everything if it got ruined.

And, well, there are a lot of “butt men” in the world, and the Shannon twins will be marketed towards them.

Karissa and Kristina won’t be the first reality stars who are also sex workers of one form or another.

They will also not be the last.

Sex work can be incredibly lucrative, and the name-recognition that stems from reality television means an automatic audience and a degree of novelty.

Of course, the twins were already modeling for Playboy when they filmed Girls Next Door.

Sometimes, career paths can loop back upon themselves.

The fact that the twins are poised to rake in a 7-figure payday is proof of how effective good branding can be.

While we’re sure that filming hardcore sex scenes alongside a sibling must be awkward as hell, they did both date Hef.
