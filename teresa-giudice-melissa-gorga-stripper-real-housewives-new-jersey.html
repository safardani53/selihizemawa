“Melissa wasn’t set up,” a close insider tells HollywoodLife.com. “The salon that the owner of the boutique did business with that day happened to have a man there that [used] to work at the same club that Melissa worked at. The guy bought a ticket.”

Teresa didn’t invite the man, nor did she ask him to confront Melissa. “He was sitting at the same table as the people from the salon and he walked up to Melissa’s table and said hello,” says the insider. “He was her boss. And she said hi and she acknowledged him.”

“When Teresa and Melissa went in the bathroom, Teresa said, ‘He said you were a dancer,'” continues the source. “Melissa flips, calls her husband. She is what she is and everybody knew it there. People who had danced with her were there.”

Teresa and the owner of the boutique had no involvement with getting Melissa’s former boss to the show. “He bought the ticket because he knows the people from the salon,” assures the source.

“First of all every one knows it,” says the insider. “Joe used to go there [to the strip club]. This was common knowledge. For him to blame the owner [and Teresa], go to the show and accost the owner … for them to act like that made them seem more guilty.”

The source adds that everything that happened as the fashion show will play a major role in the plot of upcoming season four  — and it will possibly be the focus of the season four finale!
