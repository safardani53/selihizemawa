Andrew Garfield, English-American Actor

Andrew Garfield is an English-American actor. He rose to fame in 2010 for his role as Eduardo Saverin in The Social Network. Garfield's most notable films include The Amazing Spider-Man franchise (2012-2014), 99 Homes (2014), Hacksaw Ridge (2016) and The Eyes of Tammy Faye (2021). He received a Best Actor nomination at the Academy Awards for Hacksaw Ridge in 2017. In 2022, Garfield also earned an Oscar nomination for Best Actor for tick, tick...BOOM! and won his first Golden Globe for lead actor in the musical.
