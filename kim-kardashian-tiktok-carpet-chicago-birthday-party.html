Kim Kardashian leaves TikTok users shocked after 'changing carpet' in mansion for Chicago's epic 5th

KIM Kardashian has left her fans stunned after she appeared to change the color of her carpet for a recent party.

Over the weekend, the mom-of-four threw an epic Hello Kitty-themed soirée for her daughter Chicago's fifth birthday.

The party, which was held at Kim's lavish mansion, featured indoor slides leading into a ball pit bull of pink and white balls.

There was also a real live Hello Kitty, who danced with Chicago and other revelers.

She celebrated with pink treats for every guest, a stuffed animal-making station, and an area for guests to paint their own ceramic Hello Kitty.

The Skims mogul brought in a food truck of sorts serving up "Chicago's Waffle Pops."

Kim's home was decorated with pink balloon arches and Hello Kitty balloons throughout, driving home the theme.

Chicago had a cake shaped like Hello Kitty, crudités, and a ramen bar.

There also appeared to be an array of desserts including a toast-like offering and what looked like churro-type chips with sweet dips.

However, what stood out to most was the fact that Kim had changed her nude floor to pink carpet for the special occasion.
