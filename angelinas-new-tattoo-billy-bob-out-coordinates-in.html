Billy Bob Out, Coordinates In

Oh yeah, we forgot that Angelina Jolie was actually married to Billy Bob Thornton for awhile. It appears that Shilo’s mother is doing her best to put this aspect of her past behind her, too.

At her press conference with studly husband, Brad Pitt, this week, Jolie showed up with a new tattoo. In the place of Billy Bob’s name, there’s now the following:

N11º 33′ 0″ E104º 51′ 00″
N09º 02′ 00″ E038º 45′ 00″

More magic numbers from Lost? Tomb Raider code? Or maybe just the latitude and longitude coordinates for Cambodia (son Maddox’s birthplace) and Ethiopia (daughter Zahara’s birthplace). To be honest, we’re not sure which would be stranger.

Aside from adding coordinates to for Namibia soon, perhaps Angelina could add the IP Address of The Hollywood Gossip to her collection. We’d really appreciate it.
