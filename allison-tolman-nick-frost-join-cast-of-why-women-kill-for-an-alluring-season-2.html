Allison Tolman, Nick Frost Join Cast Of Why Women Kill For An Alluring Season 2

Tolman and Frost will play a wife and husband from 1949 on the second season of the dark comedy anthology series.

Who else is ready for another round of stilettos, secrets, and self-sabotage? The next installment of dazzling dark comedy Why Women Kill is coming your way! And now we can bask in the first round of casting for Season 2, which is slated to premiere in 2021 on CBS All Access.

STREAM: All Season 1 Episodes Of Why Women Kill On CBS All Access

Allison Tolman (Fargo, Castle Rock) and Nick Frost (Fighting with My Family, Shaun of the Dead) will appear in Season 2 of the original anthology series from Creator Marc Cherry (Desperate Housewives, Devious Maids), alongside a new ensemble cast.

The dark comedy's sophomore installment will take place in 1949 and follow Alma (Tolman) and Bertram (Frost), a loving couple that faces marital drama after Alma makes a shocking discovery.

Alma, a timid and awkward housewife, remains optimistic in the face of the world’s cruelty. Her top desires in life include a spot in the local garden club and to see her ungainly daughter married, but the mundanity of her life is shattered when her husband's secret hobby is revealed.

Bertram spends his days as a veterinarian, putting sick and injured animals out of their misery. Always kind and good-humored, Bertram's hobby is darker than his jolly nature would have led one to believe.

The new season of Why Women Kill will explore the true meaning of beauty, the hidden truth behind the facades people present to the world, how being overlooked by society can affect one's psyche, and, finally, the lengths to which one woman will go in order to finally belong.

In addition to Why Women Kill, CBS All Access’ growing slate of original series currently includes The Good Fight, The Twilight Zone, Tooning Out the News, No Activity, Interrogation, The Thomas John Experience and Tell Me A Story, as well as the upcoming limited event series The Stand, That Animal Rescue Show, Texas 6, The Man Who Fell to Earth, The Harper House, For Heaven's Sake, and Guilty Party.

CBS All Access is also the exclusive domestic home to Star Trek: Discovery, Star Trek: Picard, the animated series Star Trek: Lower Decks, and the upcoming U.S.S Enterprise-set series Star Trek: Strange New Worlds.

Stay tuned for more about Season 2 of Why Women Kill, exclusively on CBS All Access.

Until then, watch Lucy Liu, Ginnifer Goodwin, and Kirby Howell-Baptiste in Season 1 of Why Women Kill, exclusively on CBS All Access.
