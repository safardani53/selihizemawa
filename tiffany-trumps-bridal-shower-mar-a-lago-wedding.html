Inside Tiffany Trump's bridal shower ahead of Mar-a-Lago wedding to Michael Boulos but sister Ivanka

TIFFANY Trump enjoyed an extravagant bridal shower this weekend hosted by her sister ahead of her upcoming nuptials in Mar-a-Lago.

Donald's youngest daughter is due to tie the knot with her billionaire fiancé Michael Boulos, 24, in front of over 500 guests on November 12.

Her half-sister Ivanka threw a pre-wedding party in Miami, Florida, to kick off the lavish celebrations just days before the wedding.

The bride-to-be donned an intricate white lace dress for the bash, complete with puff-sleeve shoulders and cut-outs at the waist.

Ivanka opted for a blue and white floral number - and spectacularly ended up clashing with her sister-in-law Lara.

The 41-year-old and her brother Eric's wife rocked up in almost identical Reformation dresses to toast Tiffany's looming big day.
