Who is Barrington Levy:

Barrington Levy is a famous Reggae Singer. He was born on April 30, 1964 and his birthplace is Jamaica. Barrington is also well known as, Reggae singer hailing from Jamaica who has released more than two dozen albums since the late 70’s. He won the award for Best Vocalist at the 1984 British Reggae Awards.

Barrington is originated from Jamaica. He and Canadian rapper K-Os were both featured on the Rascalz track “Top of the World” in 1999.
