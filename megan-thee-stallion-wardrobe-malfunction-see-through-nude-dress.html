In September, Megan Thee Stallion and Justin Timberlake appeared to get into a heated argument behind the scenes at the MTV Video Music Awards.

The rapper and singer both graced the stage separately, with Justin joining his NSync bandmates and Megan performing a song with Cardi B.

Fan accounts on X – formerly Twitter – began circulating video footage from the MTV VMAs that showed the pair in what looked like a heated exchange.

Megan was shown getting primped and ready as Justin, 42, walked by.

Suddenly, the Tennessee pop star appeared to say something to the rapper that was not audible but looked as though it upset her.

She responded – again, inaudibly – and pointed a finger in his direction before getting back to what she was doing.

He walked off but fans online were eager to know more about the unexpected disagreement.

One viewer wondered: "Did Justin Timberlake say something to piss off Megan Thee Stallion? We still haven't forgiven you for what you did to Janet Jackson. Do we have a problem?"

The post referenced the NFL Halftime performance during which Justin famously ripped off a portion of Janet's ensemble, exposing her breast on live TV.

Someone else wrote: "We don't know what Justin Timberlake said to Megan Thee Stallion, but we're on her side!"

"Did Justin Timberlake say something to piss off Megan Thee Stallion?" another viewer wondered.

It appears, however, that the argument wasn't that at all.

The father of two reportedly walked by and said, "It's so nice to meet you," to which she responded: "No, no. This don't count. This don't count. We gotta meet proper."

Another insider told the outlet: "Meg loves Justin. She was saying, 'No, no, no. We've never met before.' It was their first time meeting and she was excited."

Several online fans speculated that Justin commented on Megan's situation with rapper Tory Lanez.
