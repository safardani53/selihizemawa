GMA's Sam Champion warns host Michael Strahan is 'sharpening knives' in tense on-air 'throw down'

GOOD Morning America's Sam Champion issued a blistering warning on Friday as he braced viewers for a tense on-air "throw down."

The GMA weatherman said co-star Michael Strahan and telly chef Marcus Samuelsson were "sharpening knives" as they vowed to go head-to-head in a cook off.

Sam Champion and Heather O'Rourke judged ABC's Great Eyewitness News Holiday Cook-off with Marcus Samuelsson on Saturday.

They saw news anchors Shirleen Allicot and Ken Rosato go head-to-head in a fierce kitchen battle - but special guest Samuelsson said it was former football star Strahan he wanted to challenge next.

Speaking from his judges table, the star chef said: "I would like to call up Michael Strahan on GMA we're going to have a competition you all would like to battle.

"I bring my crew up against your crew any time. How about that?"
