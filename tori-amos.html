Tori Amos Biography

2011

Tori Amos ablum ‘Night of Hunters’

In 2011, she recorded her first classical music album, ‘Night of Hunters’, a set of tributes to greats, such as Bach, Chopin, Debussy, Granados, Satie and Schubert and also in 2012 she released ‘Gold Dust’ and formed ‘Transmission Galactic’, her own record label.
