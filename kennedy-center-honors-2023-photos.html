Photos of Queen Latifah and More Hollywood Life

It’s a night of celebration for extraordinary artists. See all the best photos from the 2023 ‘Kennedy Center Honors’ special.

The 2023 Kennedy Center Honorees include actor and comedian Billy Crystal, soprano Renée Fleming; The Bee Gees member Barry Gibb, rapper and actress Queen Latifah, and singer Dionne Warwick. Former Kennedy Center Honoree Gloria Estefan returns as host, marking her third time hosting the special.

The Kennedy Center Honors special, airing December 27 on CBS, will feature incredible tribute performances by Dove Cameron, Cynthia Erivo, Lin-Manuel Miranda, Chloe Bailey, and more. Presenters include Missy Elliott, Whoopi Goldberg, Meg Ryan, Jay Leno, and countless more. Scroll through our gallery to see all the amazing moments from the 2023 special.
