Who is Shinya:

Shinya is a famous Drummer. He was born on February 24, 1978 and his birthplace is Osaka, Japan. Shinya is also well known as, Drummer best known as a member of the Japanese visual kei metal act Dir En Grey. He has composed a number of songs for the band, including “Hotarubi,” “Umbrella” and “Raison Detre.”

Shinya is originated from Japan. He has performed alongside singer Kyo in Dir En Grey.
