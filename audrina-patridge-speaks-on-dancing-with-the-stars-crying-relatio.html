Audrina Patridge Explains Dancing With the Stars Crying, Relationship With Corey Bohan

Dancing With the Stars competitor Audrina Patridge was shown crying in rehearsal footage and being comforted by partner, Tony Dovolani, earlier this week. Why?

Because it was difficult juggling the rigorous rehearsal demands of DWTS with her relationship with Corey Bohan, the BMX standout she just started dating again.

The Hills alum says she and Corey talked it out: “Now my boyfriend understands that this means so much to me and I’m not going to let anything get in the way of it.”

Audrina and Tony quickstep into the third week.

After the show, Audzo elaborated on the waterworks: “My boyfriend and I were fighting and I was trying to come into the studio and block it all out and then Tony is telling me all these nice things and I just got a little teary-eyed.”

Audrina says training is so intense that she doesn’t want Corey to visit at the studio. “When I’m in rehearsal, I just want to focus. Little things distract me. I’m not comfortable with my clothes or anything. My focus needs to be on dance.”

 Tony told Audrina Patridge she can make time for Corey “after the season’s over. This is such a special time for her.” She agreed “this is a one-time shot. I’m discovering myself, becoming a stronger woman and Tony is helping. He’s a big brother.”
