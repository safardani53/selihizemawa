Tainy (Music Producer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Marco Masís, known by his stage name Tainy, is a Puerto Rican record producer and musician. Born and raised in San Juan, Puerto Rico, he entered the reggaeton world with Mas Flow 2. Leading a new wave of Latin music for over a decade, Tainy, is the producer of multiple hits, the world's greatest reggaeton . Several Grammy and BMI winners, Tainy has produced for countless artists including reggaeton pioneers like Daddy Yankee, Wisin & Yandel, Don Omar and others. Recently he has contributed to such global hits as Cardi B's "I Like It" with Bad Bunny and J Balvin and Balvin's "No Es Justo" with Zion & Lennox. Tainy produced Oasis with Bad Bunny and Balvin, which topped the global charts and helped Tainy hold the No. 1 Latin producer position on the Billboard charts for 27 consecutive weeks. Tainy also collaborated with Bad Bunny on the song "Callaíta" which was a huge success.
