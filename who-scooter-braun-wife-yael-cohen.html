Who is Scooter Braun's ex-wife Yael Cohen and do they have any children?

Scott "Scooter" Braun is a renowned music executive known as the manager for artists like Justin Bieber.

For seven years, he was married to Yael Cohen, a successful entrepreneur in her own right.

Who is Yael Cohen?

Cohen was born in South Africa and raised in Vancouver, British Columbia.

She is a successful entrepreneur who started the non-profit organization F*ck Cancer in 2009 after her mother was diagnosed with breast cancer.

After witnessing her first surgery, Yael decided there was a gap in the market for a campaign to target young people directly - and printed her first T-shirt with the words "F*ck Cancer."

Cohen also runs a parenting blog called Motherlucker, which aims to be "an open and honest forum for parents that is judgment-free."

In 2011, she was named by The Globe and Mail as one of 12 people who were transforming philanthropy. 

In 2018, Cohen became a senior adviser for the popular dating and networking app Bumble.

When did Yael Cohen and Scooter Braun divorce?

On July 21, 2021, it was reported that Braun filed for divorce in Los Angeles.

An insider told PEOPLE they "decided to move ahead with the divorce," adding: "They're committed to keeping things amicable for the kids. They don't want to have a messy divorce."

A year later, their second baby, Levi Magnus Braun, was born.

On December 1, 2018, Cohen and Braun had their first daughter, Hart Violet.
