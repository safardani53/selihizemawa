Todays Craig Melvin mocked by co-hosts over embarrassing blunder during live interview with Yello

TODAY show host Craig Melvin has fans and co-hosts alike mocking him for an on-air blunder.

The 43-year-old chatted with Yellowstone star Luke Grimes about his role in the Western drama on Thursday's episode.

"It's one of my favorite shows," Craig gushed. "Just love watching it. You're my favorite character, and I'm not just saying that because you're sitting here."

"Are you fangirling right now?" co-host Dylan Dreyer asked Craig, as he sputtered that he was "watching these clips" as footage from Yellowstone played during the discussion.

But then the clearly flustered Craig made a bit of a blunder right after that.

"But Kayce, I mean, do you watch the show?" Craig asked Luke, referring to him by his name in the show, Kayce Dutton.

"Did you just call him Kayce?" Dylan asked Craig as both she and Sheinelle Jones busted out laughing.

"It happens all the time," Luke assured Craig with an easygoing grin. "It's fine."

Immediately after calling Luke "Kayce," Craig did it again, but the TV star was unfazed.

At the end of the segment, Craig was apologetic for calling him by the wrong name.

All this comes as Today fans were thrilled to see the return of beloved weatherman Al Roker to the morning show on Friday.

Savanna and Hoda Kotb introduced Al as he made his way onstage.

The pair held back tears as they embraced Al.

"It's great to be back," he said, while holding their hands. "And to be wearing pants!"

Al had been missing from the morning show since the beginning of November due to extensive health issues.
