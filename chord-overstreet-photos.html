Photos Of The Actor &amp; Singer Hollywood Life

Chord Overstreet is an actor and country singer best known for his role as Sam Evans on the hit television series Glee. He’s also appeared in episodes of series such as The Middle, Regular Show, and No Ordinary Family. 

Before fame, he grew up in Nashville, TN, with his mother Julie, a make-up artist, and Paul Overstreet, a country musician and songwriter. Chord and his five siblings were raised in a creative, music-filled environment. He started playing multiple instruments at a young age, including mandolin, drums, flute, piano, and guitar, which came in handy during his time on Glee. His older brother, Nash Overstreet, is in the band Hot Chelle Rae. 

In 2011, 2012, and 2013, Chord was nominated for a Screen Actors Guild Award for his work on Glee. 

He previously dated Emma Roberts and briefly dated actress Emma Watson in the summer of 2018.
