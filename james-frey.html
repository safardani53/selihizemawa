Who is James Frey:

James Frey is a famous Novelist. He was born on September 12, 1969 and his birthplace is Ohio. James is also well known as, Novelist and memoirist who first gained national attention from the acclaim and subsequent controversy surrounding his 2003 memoir A Million Little Pieces. He has also served as the CEO of Full Fathom Five, the production company responsible for the popular Lorien Legacies series of young adult science fiction books.

James is originated from United States. As claims of factual inaccuracies within A Million Little Pieces began to mount, he appeared with his mother on Larry King Live to defend himself and his work.
