Jennifer Lopez Rocks White String Bikini Top in St. Barts: Photos Hollywood Life

Winter is in full swing and stars like Jennifer Lopez, 54, are fleeing to warmer climates! Jennifer accessorized with a few beach essentials. In the photos (seen here), the brunette bombshell added a wide-brim sunhat, oversized sunglasses, and a few gold bracelets to her ensemble. J.Lo wore her giant diamond wedding ring from hubby Ben Affleck. While she enjoyed her vacation, the mother-of-two was seemingly still plugged in and was spotted chatting on the phone with her wired headphones.

After photos of her bikini moment in St. Barts landed on social media, Jennifer’s fans took to the comments to react. “Bummer, I’m not there as usual,” one admirer joked, while another tweeted, “Wow @JLo is so beautiful.” A separate fan called Jennifer’s look “Effortlessly chic.” J.Lo was seemingly sans Ben during her R&R moment, as he was not pictured by her side that afternoon.

She completed her plunging swimsuit with gold hoop earrings and a matching gold nameplate necklace.
