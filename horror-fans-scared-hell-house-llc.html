Horror fans scared to be home alone and cant sleep after watching nightmare fuel clow

HORROR movie fans say Hell House LLC has made them terrified to be home alone.

The clown film - written and directed by Stephen Cognetti - stars actors Gore Abrams, Alice Bahlke, Danny Bellini.

The cast worked to create a tense and creepy atmosphere that's left reviewers too scared to sleep.

The film is set five years after an unexplained malfunction causes the death of 15 people on the opening night of a Halloween haunted house tour.

To mark the anniversary, a documentary crew goes back to the scene of the tragedy to find out what really happened.

It was first released back in 2015 but has only just started streaming on Amazon Prime.

The reviews flooded in to praise the horror for its "genuinely surprising and scary moments".

One said: "Great fun film as long as you don't like sleep."

Another claimed: "Holy s*** was I afraid to go to sleep last night."

"It was actually very scary and I don’t scare easily. A pleasant surprise," said another.
