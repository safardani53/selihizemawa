Josh Dallas’s Age & Early Life

Joshua Paul Dallas was born on December 18, 1978, in Kentucky, United States. Robert Micheal Dallas is his father, while Diana Raymond is his mother. He has one sibling. He graduated from Mountview Academy of Theater Arts with a Sarah Exley Scholarship after attending New Albany High School. Dallas is of All-American ancestry.

Career

Josh Dallas went on to study at the Royal Shakespeare Company, the Royal National Theater, the English National Opera, and The Young Vic after graduating. In 2006, he made his professional debut in the action-drama series Ultimate Force. In 2008, he had an appearance in the BBC television show Doctor Who. The next year, Dallas played Ace in the television film The Last Days of Lehman Brothers.

Between 2010 and 2011, the actor appeared in various television shows, including Money, Hawaii Five-O, CSI, and Five. From 2011 to 2018, he starred as Prince Charming/David Nolan in the ABC fantasy television program Once Upon A Time, where he rose to fame. He has been starring as Ben Stone in the supernatural television series Manifest since 2018. Dallas made his feature film debut in the German action thriller 80 Minutes. In 2011, he played Fandral in Marvel’s Thor. He also appeared in the films Red Tails and Sidekick. In addition to acting, he has provided voice work for films such as The Boxer, The Descent 2, Ghost Machine, and Zootopia.

Josh Dallas Wife & Marriage

Who is Josh Dallas’s Wife? Josh Dallas is a husband. On April 12, 2014, he married the stunning actress Ginnifer Goodwin in California. They began dating in 2011 during the production of Once Upon a Time and got engaged in October 2013. This couple has two children: Oliver Finlay Dallas, born in May 2014, and Hugo Wilson Dallas, born in June of 2016. Josh previously married actress Lara Pulver in 2007, but the couple split in 2011.
