Neymar PSG shirt: French champs charging staggering 140 EACH for No 10 replica shirts with huge qu

PARIS SAINT-GERMAIN fans are in a Neymar frenzy – and snapping up shirts for £140 with the star’s signature on his back.

The Brazilian finally completed his move from Barcelona to the French capital on Thursday.

PSG have paid a world-record £198million to bring the 25-year-old to the Parc des Princes.

The transfer saga eventually came to an end yesterday when the Samba Boy penned a five-year deal with Unai Emerey’s side.

PSG confirmed the move last night and will unveil their new talisman at 12.30pm this afternoon.

And fans could not wait to get their side’s new shirts with Neymar’s name on the back… even if it left them out of pocket.

Respected Sky Sports journalist Bryan Swanson claims that at least one supporter was first on the scene at the club’s store – and paid the whopping figure to get his new hero on the back of his jersey.

And not content with one, the fan actually purchased four shirts with the name and number of their latest signing on the back.

THE LATEST ON THE NEYMAR MEGA-MOVE

Purchasing your team’s colours can be an expensive business these days.

However, in the rush to be a trendsetter and be amongst the first to get Neymar on his shirt, the male found himself seriously out of pocket.

If he had decided to wait, he could have go online and visited PSG’s official club store and pocketed himself a fortune.

They are selling the new attire with Neymar’s name and number 10 number donned on the back for £82.95.

Even if he had decided to get the official Ligue 1 badge on the arms for an extra fiver, he would still have netted a decent saving.
