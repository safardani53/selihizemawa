England star Jack Grealish leaves club with brunette beauty after a wild night out in Las Vegas

ENGLAND star Jack Grealish has been spotted leaving a club with a brunette after a wild night in Las Vegas.

The Manchester City ace went on the lash until gone 3am before boarding a hired bus with the girl.

An onlooker said: “They were clearly enjoying each other’s company.”

Grealish enjoyed an £80,000 VIP package including 116 bottles of champagne as he toasted Man City’s title win on a Las Vegas lads’ holiday.

The Three Lions favourite invited a brunette to join his party bus after they chatted and giggled on a dancefloor until 3.30am.

“The group were sinking beers, chanting songs and whooping and hollering. It was classic ‘Brits abroad’ stuff.

The deal also included a snap of Jack’s group beamed on to the side of a high-rise tower.

The two-level apartment features ultra-high ceilings and stunning views of the Vegas skyline and the hotel’s golf course.
