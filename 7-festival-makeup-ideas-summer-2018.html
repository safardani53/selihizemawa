7 festival makeup ideas for 2018...from glitter everywhere to gold foil eyelids

FORGET the music and partying in a field, one of the most exciting things about festival season is the big reveal of the summer’s more ‘out there’ beauty trends.

Last summer was all about taking the usual facial glitter and applying it to your hair parting, but what are the festival trends of 2018?

Gold foil eyelids

Glitter eyeshadow has been a festival makeup staple forever.

but popping up on Instagram this summer gold foil eyeshadow - which takes your typical glitter up a few notches. 

But while it looks totally extra, the gold eye foil can be expensive - Stila are selling theirs at £33.50.

Coloured mascara

It’s official, coloured mascara is back.

And now the trend has been spotted at festivals from Coachella to London’s newest, All Points East.

For the best effect, opt for chunky lashes in bright pinks, blues, purples and yellows.

Multi-toned lips

This year, the lip trend is not dissimilar to the eyeshadow style - and it’s all about multi-tones.

Whether your colour scheme is pinks, blues or a full on rainbow, pucker up and apply different shades.

Go big, go bold.

Graphic liner

Drawing a single sweep of floating liner midway up your eyelid is a striking look.

You’ll see the trend on Instagram a lot this summer, with celebs, influencers and bold festival goers giving it a whirl.

Take things further by adding patterns to your sweeping line.

Mismatched eyeshadow

Applying colourful, mismatched eyeshadow above and below your eyes is a quick way to look festival ready.

Opt for the same blend above both eyes, and set this off by using a different colour scheme beneath.

For example, try a pink on your eyelids and an orange beneath, or go all out and create a rainbow effect.

Abstract shapes

Even bolder still is this year’s trend for drawing abstract shapes (in makeup) on your face.

Essentially, this is your chance to make your face a piece of art - squares, rectangles and lines all welcome.

Use eyeliner, glitter gel or other skin-safe products and see where your creativity takes you.

Glitter everywhere

Whether it’s a protest against following the crowd with your festival makeup or simply the work of drunk appliance, ‘glitter everywhere’ is now a cool look.

How do you get it? You literally apply glitter all over your face - with no concern for symmetry or attempts to outline your bone structure.

What’s more, you can even adopt a similar look while getting ready with Glamglow’s glitter My Little Pony face mask.
