Cardi B's daughter Kulture, 3, wears $2.4K Balenciaga outfit to Disney after rapper slammed for kids

CARDI B's daughter Kulture went designer for Disneyland, wearing a $2.4K Balenciaga outfit - not counting her $500K watch.

The three-year-old's rapper mom was slammed for buying her expensive clothes ahead of their trip.

Cardi B, 29, shared new photos and a video to her Instagram page with her Disney-ready daughter DRAPED in designer duds.

"Light but heavy," the rapper captioned her post.

Kulture smiled and posed in the snaps as she wore her hair in a bun and Balenciaga head-to-toe.

It included $550 Balenciaga shoes, a tiny beige $350 logo hoodie, $250 matching sweatpants, a $195 “Los Angeles” tee-shirt and a $1K pint-sized alligator “Hourglass” bag - totaling $2.4K and that's before the watch.

Kulture completed the cute but expensive look from Kim Kardashian's go-to brand with diamond earrings, three chunky sparkling chains a Patek Philippe watch her mom gifted her for turning two, per an earlier Instagram.

The diamond-teeming timepiece can sell for six figures and prices dial up to $500K.

Kulture cutely utilized her watch in a video in the Instagram slideshow:

"Mommy we're late, it's six o'clock" she noted.

BIRKIN BAGS & DIAMONDS

This is far from Kulture’s first fashion flaunting.

She appeared on the cover of Vogue at only 17 months old, and received a rainbow-bedazzled Hermès Birkin bag worth $48K last year, per Page Six.

For her birthday in July 2021, she was gifted a diamond Minnie Mouse necklace from her dad and Cardi's husband, Migos rapper Offset.

It features five charms shaped like an Hermès Birkin bag, a Chanel logo, Minnie Mouse, a bow-wrapped heart and a “K” initial (for Kulture, one could assume).

Though that price tag is unknown, when she turned 1, she received a $100,000 chain adorned with colorful characters from the Netflix series “Word Party,” per Page Six.

Unrelated to the designer duds, but Cardi and Kulture's Disney trip went viral when security members were seen enjoying the teacup ride.

Cardi and Offset tied the knot in 2017 and welcomed their Kulture in July 2018.

In 2021 they welcomed their now five-month-old son.

It has been reported that Cardi is worth an estimated $40million, a huge portion from her music and her OnlyFans career.
