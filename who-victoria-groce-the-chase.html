Who is Victoria Groce on The Chase?

ABC'S THE Chase follows teams of three contestants who attempt to win money by challenging a quiz expert, also known as a Chaser.

Amongst the Chasers on season 3 is Victoria Groce and here's everything we know about her.

Who is Victoria Groce on The Chase?

Victoria Groce is a quiz expert hailing from Georgia.

Prior to Groce's involvement on The Chase, she made a notable appearance in 2005 when she starred on Jeopardy!.

During her time on the show, she was able to knock down David Madden's 19-day streak.

In 2021, Groce was named World Quizzer of the Year. She additionally played on the winning team for the 2021-2022 Quizzing World Cup.

As of July 2023, Groce resides in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania where she lives with her husband and daughter.

Why is Victoria Groce called The Queen?

Victoria Groce has been rightfully named, "The Queen," due to her domination in the competitive quiz community.

Groce has competed individually and in a team across three continents.

When introducing Groce on The Chase, the show's host, Sara Haines introduced the Georgia native in a royal manner.

Haines said: "This is the smartest quizzer on the globe and she has no intention of giving up her title, or her crown. Fear her Majesty!"

What does Victoria Groce do for a living?

Apart from being a regal quiz expert, Groce works as a freelance writer for Verywell Health.

Groce normally touches on topics such as restricted diet and science features.

Additionally, Groce worked as a freelance editor for university presses.

According to ABC, Groce also enjoys "cooking unnecessarily elaborated meals, kayaking, and knitting" in her downtime.
