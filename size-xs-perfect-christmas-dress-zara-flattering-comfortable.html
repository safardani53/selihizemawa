A FASHION fan has revealed her holy grail of holiday dresses, just in time for Christmas.

She said her festive frock is not only cut in a flattering style — it's comfortable, too.

Sam Jackson (@samstyle__) filmed herself trying on and accessorizing the dress in a video on TikTok.

"The most perfect Christmas dress from Zara," she said.

"I'm literally obsessed with this, its so flattering and super comfortable. My potential Christmas day dress," she added.

She donned a glitzy black dress, pairing it with black knee-high boots, earrings, and a small handbag.

Although she clearly enjoyed the dress, she mentioned one regret when purchasing it.

"I should’ve gotten an XS babe. [It] was quite big," she said.

The video drew in many fans of the look who shared their thoughts in the comment section.

"Really beautiful," one wrote.

The dress is called the Shoulder Pads Sequin Dress, retailing for $89.90 from Zara.

According to Zara, the dress boasts a unique design for an elevated fit.

"Round neck sleeveless mini dress with shoulder pads. Tonal sequin appliqué. Back hidden in-seam zip closure," the description reads.

The video came as part of a social media trend where fashion fans share their favorite holiday looks.

With Christmas around the corner, partygoers are rushing to purchase last-minute dresses.

Many designs and popular color palettes include more than the standard red or green, incorporating sparkle to shine during the most wonderful time of the year.
