Fashion fans go wild for Primark's new lingerie sets that are a total dupe for Lounge undies... and

FASHION fans are running to Primark after spotting dupes of Lounge underwear sets for just £6.

Cal took to TikTok to share a video of the racks of bright lacy bra and thong sets in the high street store, writing: "Primark Lounge Underwear dupe."

The clip began by showing the hot pink lace underwired bra and thong, which featured the words "Love to Lounge" on the elastic band underneath the cups.

"Coupled up perfectly with the coordinating thong which combines a flattering cut with barely-there fit for all day comfort, both pieces feature 'love to lounge' elastic and are ideal whether you're lounging, loving, or out living life to the fullest!" Primark wrote in the product description on their website.

The Lounge Blossom Balcony Bra and Thong sets, which come in at £45, simply say "Lounge".

As well as the lace sets, which also come in orange and green, there's a grey ribbed set with a moulded cup bra and thong for £8.
