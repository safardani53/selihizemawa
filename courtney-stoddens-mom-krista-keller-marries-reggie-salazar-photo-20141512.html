Courtney Stodden's Mom Krista Keller Marries Reggie Salazar: Photo

Momager's married! Courtney Stodden's mom and manager Krista Keller got hitched to Reggie Salazar this past Sunday, Dec. 14, in L.A., the blushing bride has revealed exclusively to Us Weekly.

"I am very happy that my beautiful daughter Courtney Stodden walked me down the aisle on this very special day," Keller told Us of the occasion. "I am honored that after 56 years of my husband's life of being single, he chose me to be his bride. We had a beautiful wedding ceremony and are looking forward to our lives together."

This is the second marriage for Keller, who gave Us a glimpse of the joyous family event. In the wedding pic, Keller wears a dark lilac silk gown with a cutout above her chest. Her new hubby, the president and founder of Pasadena-based startup Powersharing, beams beside her in a suit, while Courtney leans beside her mom on the opposite side.

Also joining the family are several pals, as well as 20-year-old Courtney's no-longer-estranged husband Doug Hutchison, 54.
