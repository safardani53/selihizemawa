Megan Fox Goes Blonde for Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Photos

Holy blonde bombshell alert!

She’s best-known for her long brunette tresses, but Megan Fox ditched her natural hue and signature length on the set of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles 2 on Thursday, April 30. Say hello to a bright blonde Fox with a bob!

Working a tousled, honey-hued wig, which just brushed her shoulders, the actress, 27, filmed a scene in Grand Central Terminal in NYC. She sported glasses, a button-up blouse, and a pencil skirt in character as April O’Neil. She also rocked a hot pink statement lip.

In a second photo, Fox, carrying her wig in hand (and proving definitively that she did not dye!), strutted her stuff through what appeared to be a Halloween party. For the fun scene, the mom of two modeled a look that was very Britney Spears circa “…Baby One More Time,” complete with a knotted top and a schoolgirl skirt.

While Fox, who’s been married to Brian Austin Green since 2010, has never tried a full-on blonde ‘do, she has experimented with color in the past. The raven-haired beauty embraced the ombre trend in 2013 and 2014. She’s also tinted her hair red for TMNT2, as she did for the previous installment.
