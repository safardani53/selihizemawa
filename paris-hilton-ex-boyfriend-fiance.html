Paris Hilton ex-boyfriend list: How many times has the heiress been engaged?

RUMOURS have stated that American socialite Paris Hilton is expecting her first child.

The heiress became engaged to her boyfriend, businessman Carter Reum in February, 2021. This isn't the first time she hopes to make her way to the altar.

Who has the heiress dated and been engaged to?

Chris Zylka

Paris and HBO star Chris Zylka started dating in 2017. He proposed to her in 2018 during a ski trip to Aspen, Colorado.

"My best friend & soulmate,” she wrote at the time. “Perfect for me in every way.” 

The pair split after a two-year engagement in November 2018.

"I'm just really having my 'me time'", Paris went on to the say on CBS' The Talk, following their break up.

"I just feel that when I fall in love, I fall in love fast and hard and it was this whirlwind romance."

Thomas Gross

The Hilton heiress and businessman Thomas Gross dated for one year, after meeting at the Cannes Film Festival in May, 2015.

Paris was pictured in his company on a yacht off the coast of Spain. The couple were then sharing a kiss but kept a low profile.

In a November, 2015 interview, Paris told Closer that Thomas "is very private so he'd never want cameras following [them] around."

"I'm so busy with my work that when I do get the time at home, I don't have time to watch reality TV. I like to cook for my boyfriend and watch movies together."

They called it quits on their relationship in April 2016.

River Viiperi

Paris and Ibiza-born model River Viiperi dated for two years.

She confirmed their break up after she was spotted kissing a mystery man in Malibu in 2014.

Afrojack

World famous DJ Afrojack and Paris Hilton sparked dating rumors in 2012, but he denied the pair were ever an item,

“100 percent not accurate,” a rep for the reality star told Us at the time. 

"Paris and Afrojack are best friends and love and support each other very much.”

Cy Waits

Actor Cy Waits and Paris got together in 2010, but split after a year of dating.

“We're not together anymore," Hilton confirmed on a late night talk show at the time.

"I love him and we remain really good friends ... I feel like the relationship ran its course and we'll see what happens."

Doug Reinhardt

Paris and Doug Reinhardt, both reality stars, began dating in 2009 but Paris pulled the plug on their relationship after a year of dating.

"I am like so past that,” Hilton told Us Weekly one week after their split. 

“I don't even care. I don't even remember that time in my life. I am over it!"

Benji Madden

Paris and the Good Charlotte guitarist, who is now married to Cameron Diaz, dated for nine months in 2008.

"She is saddened by the break up but they're just too different and they wanted different things in life", a source close to Paris told People.

"He was loyal and sweet but it was time to take a break."

Paris Latsis

Paris and Paris started dating in December 2004, and got engaged eight months later.

They called off their wedding in September 2005.

"I'm sad to announce that I've called off my engagement", Paris wrote in a statement breaking the news.

"Over the last couple of months, I've realized that this is the right decision for me. We remain best of friends, and I'll always love him."

Stavros Niarchos

After Paris and Paris Latsis split up, she began seeing Stavros Niarchos, son of a Greek tycoon billionaire.

The pair had an on-and-off relationship, but things ended for good in 2007.

Nick Carter

Backstreet Boy alum Nick Carter and Paris dated for seven months before they called time on their relationship in July 2004.

In his autobiography published 2013, Aaron opened up about his relationship with Paris. “Paris was the worst person in the world for me to hook up with,” he wrote. “[She] fed my worst impulses as far as partying.”

Rick Salomon

In 2003, Rick Salomon famously leaked a sex tape he had recorded with the former reality star titled One Night in Paris.

“I just felt so betrayed,” Hilton said of the scandal during an interview in 2011.

“This was not some random guy; this was someone I was with for a few years.”

Jason Shaw

Jason Shaw and Paris were engaged for a year between 2002-2003.

The two reportedly remained friends after their split, and even sparked rekindling rumors in 2010.

Paris has always loved Jason and cared about him deeply," E! News reported at the time. "But when they were together she was just a kid and not ready for such a commitment."

Carter Reum

The Hilton family heiress and her beau were engaged in February, 2021, after a year of dating.

Carter proposed to Paris on a private island with an emerald-cut diamond ring designed by Jean Dousset.

A source told PageSix that Paris, 40, was pregnant with future husband Carter, also 40.

The socialite revealed in February that she had decided to begin IVF treatments as she wanted to expand her family later in life.
