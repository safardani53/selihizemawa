New Sylvester Stallone Docuseries, The Family Stallone, Coming To Paramount Plus

Premiering this spring, the new series follows the Hollywood legend and his family.

Get ready to see Oscar-nominee Sylvester Stallone in his most important role yet: Dad!

The Family Stallone, a new docuseries following Sylvester "Sly" Stallone, his wife Jennifer Flavin Stallone, and their daughters Sophia, Sistine, and Scarlet, will premiere this spring, exclusively on Paramount+. 

The eight-part series offers a seat at the table of one of Hollywood's most famous families.

Meet the family Stallone:

- Sylvester Stallone: While Sly stars in box office hits, at home he's focused on building for the future and raising three independent and ambitious women.

- Jennifer Flavin Stallone: Born and raised in Los Angeles, Jennifer is a savvy businesswoman and co-owner of the wellness brand Serious Skin Care.

- Sophia: The eldest of the three Stallone sisters, Sophia hosts a podcast with her sister Sistine. She has no interest in following her father's acting footsteps and is still finding her own path in life.

- Sistine: An actress and model, Sistine is embarking on a new journey as a filmmaker and currently developing her first feature film.

- Scarlet: The youngest Stallone sister and the last to leave the nest, Scarlet is moving into her own apartment at college. She's wanted to be an actress since she was a child and now acts alongside Sly in his new series Tulsa King, also available on Paramount+.

Stay tuned for more information on The Family Stallone, including premiere date, at ParamountPlus.com.
