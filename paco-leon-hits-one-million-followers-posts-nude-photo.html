Paco Len Hits One Million Followers, Posts Nude Photo

Paco León is a man of his word.

And also a man with a rather nice physique.

The Spanish comedian and actor promised last week that he would post a photo of himself entirely nude if he hit one million followers.

So once that figure was achieved, the 38-year old went right ahead and posed in nothing but his birthday suit, including with the image a simple caption: “You asked for it, I promised you and here it is: #weare1million.”

León, best known for his role in the comedy Aída, looks good and all. But there’s only one proper response to this picture:
