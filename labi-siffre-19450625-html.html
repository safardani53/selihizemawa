Claudius Afolabi Siffre is an English singer, songwriter, musician and poet. Siffre released six albums between 1970 and 1975, and four albums from 1988 to 1998. His most famous compositions include "It Must Be Love", "Crying Laughing Loving Lying" and "So Strong". His music has been widely sampled by hip-hop artists including Eminem, Jay-Z and Kanye West. Siffre has published essays, TV plays and plays, Deathwrite, and three volumes of poetry: Nigger, Blood on the Page and Monument.
