Meet Hollywood's oldest new dads as Al Pacino, 83, and Robert De Niro, 79, welcome babies in twiligh

IT seems more and more Hollywood hotshots are taking the phrase "dear old dad" to new levels.

Earlier this week, 83-year-old iconic actor Al Pacino shocked fans when he announced that his 29-year-old girlfriend Noor Alfallah is eight months pregnant.

The couple has been together since April 2022 and now they'll be linked for the rest of his life.

A rep from Al's team confirmed that the Oscar winner's girlfriend, who is 54 years her baby daddy's junior, is already eight months into her pregnancy, and is expected to give birth any day.

The Godfather actor already has three children - two with Beverly D'Angelo and one with Jan Tarrant.

Noor previously dated legendary musician Mick Jagger, and works in the film industry.

She studied film in college, getting her undergraduate degree from the University of Southern California’s School of Cinematic Arts, and her master’s degree in Film and TV producing from the University of California-Los Angeles.

She's known for producing the films Billy Knight, Little Death, and Brosa Nostra.

By the time their baby graduates high school, Al will be 100 years old. But he's hardly the first old dad to welcome a new child in his twilight years.

ROBERT DE NIRO

Al's long time friend Robert De Niro also had a child late in life at the age of 79.

Last month, Robert revealed in a shocking interview that he had become a father once again.

The Rocky and Bullwinkle star was asked by a reporter from ET Canada what it was like to be a father of six children.

The Oscar winner corrected the reporter, saying: "Seven, actually. I just had a baby."

He did not divulge the baby's gender or name.

The Goodfellas actor also kept the identity of his baby's mother a secret.

MICK JAGGER

Mick Jagger proved he had gathered no moss when the Rolling Stone legend welcomed a son in 2016.

Mick was 73 at the time.

The Satisfaction crooner was already a father of seven when he met former ballerina Melanie Hamrick in 2014.

Two years later, the couple welcomed son Deveraux Octavian Basil.

A spokesperson for the couple released a statement at the time after Deveraux's arrival: "Melanie Hamrick and Mick Jagger's son was born today in New York and they are both delighted.

"Mick was at the hospital for the arrival. Mother and baby are doing well and we request that the media respect their privacy at this time."

RICHARD GERE

Pretty Woman star Richard Gere became a pretty old dad in 2020, when he welcomed a baby boy at 71.

Richard's wife, publicist Alejandra Silva, had given birth to their first child together just two years earlier.

They were seen vacationing with the tot in Italy in the summer, with him bouncing the child up and down on his knee during a boat trip.

Alejandra revealed she was pregnant with their first child in dramatic fashion in 2018, by getting the Dalai Lama to bless her baby bump.

Posting a picture of the pair with the Tibetan Buddhist leader, she wrote: "We couldn't announce it before telling HH Dalai Lama."

DAVID FOSTER

Music producer David Foster was 71 when he and wife Katharine McPhee welcomed their first son in 2021.

A source close to the couple confirmed at the time that the American Idol alum and her husband were doing well and the baby was healthy.

Before delivering, Katharine told the Women on Top podcast of expecting a son with her husband, David Foster: "Since I'm having a boy, I don't know if people know that, it's definitely different, you think about different things."

David is a Canadian musician and producer who has worked with industry legends including Alice Cooper, Rod Stewart, and Kenny Rogers. 

The couple first met when he mentored her on season five of American Idol in 2006.

They married in June 2019 at the St Yeghiche Armenian Church in London.

ALEC BALDWIN

Alec Baldwin was 64 when he and his wife, Hilaria, welcomed their seventh child in 2022.

The baby girl was named Ilaria Catalina Irena.

In an Instagram video, Hilaria had tears in her eyes as she held her infant daughter in her arms.

Alec, 64, also made a brief appearance in the video.

He held up his arm and displayed his daughter's footprint.

The yoga instructor captioned her post: "She’s here! We are so excited to introduce you to our tiny dream come true."

She continued: "Both she and I are happy and healthy.

"Her Baldwinito siblings are spending the day bonding and welcoming her into our home."

She concluded: "Much love to you all. We are so happy to celebrate this wonderful news with you."

NIKKI SIXX

Motley Crue rocker Nikki Sixx was Nikky 60 when he welcomed his fifth child with wife Courtney in 2019.

A source at the time said: "[Nikki] is such a great father already, and she is going to be the best mom. She is so caring."

The legendary musician became a superstar in the 80s after founding Los Angeles band Motley Crue.

Famed for their tales of excess and debauchery, the band laid bare their exploits in the 2001 book The Dirt.

Courtney married Nikki at the Greystone Estate in Los Angeles in 2014 after a friend set her up with the star.

The bassist has four additional children from past marriages to Brandi Brandt and Donna D'Errico.

RANDALL EVERRETT

Vanderpump Rules star Lala Kent and her now ex-fiance Randall Emmett welcomed their daughter in 2021.

The film producer was 50.

Ocean Kent was Lala's first child, though Randall already shared two children from his previous marriage to actress Ambyr Childers.

Lala and Randall split in October 2021 after being engaged for three years, following accusations that he cheated on her with multiple women.

Ambyr has made several attempts at obtaining a restraining order against Randall, but they have been dismissed - the last one was dismissed at the end of January.

Ambyr and Lala have allegedly joined forces against Randall after becoming close friends.

Earlier this year, Randall said despite the ongoing feuds, he is focused on being "best bada** dad on the planet."
