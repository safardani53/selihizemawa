Did Steven Barkley Coach In The NFL? Warren Mott Coach Wife And Son

Steven Barkley, a seasoned high school football coach with decades of experience, has yet to coach in the NFL.

Beyond his extensive career on the football field, Barkley enjoys a blissful marriage spanning a decade with his wife.

In the tapestry of his personal life, the crowning jewel is his son, Drew, who is the most fulfilling part of his life.

Originally from Roseville, Michigan, Steven Barkley now resides in Warren, Michigan.

He earned his BA degree in communication from Concordia University Chicago in 2001.

Subsequently, he pursued his MA Degree in School Administration and Special Education at the University of Detroit Mercy.

In 2012, Barkley furthered his education at Northcentral University, achieving a Doctor of Education in Educational Leadership and Administration.

NFL Aspirations and Multifaceted Endeavors: The Diverse Professional Journey of Steven Barkley

Steven Barkley wears multiple hats in his professional life, serving as the owner and CEO of Steven Barkley – State Farm Agent, where his insurance expertise shields State Farm customers from various risks.

In addition to his corporate responsibilities, Barkley has dedicated the past two decades to coaching the Warren Mott High School football team.

Since joining in 2003, he has been the head coach for the 9th-grade squad, showcasing his commitment to both the sport and the development of young athletes.

Despite his extensive coaching background, including over 20 years in football, Barkley has yet to make a leap to the NFL.

Beyond the gridiron, he contributes to the security team at Fifth Avenue Royal Oak.

He has undertaken this role since 2011, accumulating over a decade of dedicated service.

This isn’t his first security role; he brings a wealth of experience from previous positions at Reunion Night Club, The Crofoot Ballroom, Clutch Cargos, and Hot Rocks Night Club.

Furthermore, Barkley has a history of contributing to education, having previously worked as a teacher at Roseville Community School, Fitzgerald Public School, and Warren Consolidated School.

His diverse professional background reflects a commitment to both community and personal growth.

Love, Family, and Sports: The Barkley Journey with Crystal and Drew

Steven Barkley is happily married to Crystal Barkley, who serves as an agency field specialist at State Farm.

Their journey as a couple began on May 26, 2012, and has flourished for more than a decade.

Two years into their marital bliss, the Barkleys were joyously joined by their son, Drew, in 2014.

The couple, ever-proud parents, regularly share glimpses of their cherished moments with Drew on social media, reflecting the deep affection they have for their family.

Steven Barkley’s social media handle provides a delightful window into the family’s active lifestyle.

Young Drew is frequently captured engaging in various sports, such as football, soccer, skating, and more.

It’s evident that Barkley is not just a coach but a father who instills a passion for sports in his son.

Moreover, Barkley goes the extra mile, often taking Drew along to sporting events like golfing and weight lifting.

This intentional involvement underscores Barkley’s commitment to instilling a love for sports and a sporting mindset in his son, Drew.
