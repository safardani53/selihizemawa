Alyse Eady Was Adopted As Child

Born on 15 March 1988 at Fort-Smith, Arkansas Alyse Cynthia Eady was an adopted child who had a difficult childhood. Both of her foster parents were ill as she was growing up and she had to take the role of a caretaker.

Her father Lewis went blind, and her mother Lady had lupus. She is very thankful, loving and caring towards her parents and often shares her love through pictures on social media.

She graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Mass and Speech Communication from Ouachita Baptist University. Later she also pursued Masters of Science in Communication and Media Studies from Purdue University.

Alyse Eady Was Famous As Beauty Pageant Holder Before Being Fox 5's News Anchor

The tall and gorgeous Eady with a towering height of 5’8 inches, gained fame after she won Miss Teen Arkansas-America in 2004. In 2010 she returned back to the pageant world by joining Miss America and bagged the first-runner-up title with $25,000 scholarship.

The title enhanced her fame in the media and n 2011 she also appeared on Late Night Show With David Letterman.
