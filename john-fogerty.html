Who is John Fogerty:

John Fogerty is a famous Rock Singer. He was born on May 28, 1945 and his birthplace is Berkeley, CA. John is also well known as, Singer and guitarist for Creedence Clearwater Revival, best known for hits such as “Fortunate Son” and “Have You Ever Seen The Rain.” Fogerty later pursued a successful solo career. Fogerty announced in 2012 that he was writing a memoir about his career. 

John is originated from United States. He was once in The Blue Velvets with Doug Clifford.
