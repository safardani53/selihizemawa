Who is Sonny Curtis:

Sonny Curtis is a famous Country Singer. He was born on May 9, 1937 and his birthplace is Texas. Sonny is also well known as, Country and pop singer who gained fame while he was a member of the band The Crickets. His first album was 1964’s Beatle Hits Flamenco Style Guitar.

Sonny is originated from United States. Curtis was teenage friends with musician Buddy Holly and joined the band The Crickets after Holly’s death.
