Who is Todd Rundgren:

Todd Rundgren is a famous Rock Singer. He was born on June 22, 1948 and his birthplace is Upper Darby, PA. Todd is also well known as, Musical innovator known for such hits as “Hello It’s Me,” “I Saw the Light,” and “Bang the Drum All Day.” He was a pivotal figure in power pop in the 1970’s and 1980’s.

Todd is originated from United States. In the 1990’s, he toured with former Beatles member Ringo Starr.
