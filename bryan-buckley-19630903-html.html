Bryan Buckley is an American filmmaker, screenwriter, producer and director who has been nominated for an Oscar twice. His successful career earned him the title of “King of the Super Bowl.” Director Buckley first created a series of commercials for the NHL on ESPN, before making a fight translated “This is SportsCenter” for ESPN in 1999. The work became part of the ESPN brand and brought Buckley into the commercial world. Buckley will continue directing 64 Super Bowl commercials. He has directed two feature films and four short films. In his four short films, Krug performed at Sundance, Wake Up Caller performed at Tribeca, while ASAD and SARIA were nominated for best Live Action Short at the Oscars. His two nominations in the same decade are the first time a director has returned to the category in more than 30 years.
