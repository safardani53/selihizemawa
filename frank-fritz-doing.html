What happened to Frank Fritz and how is he doing now?

FRANK FRITZ was found on the floor by a friend after suffering a stroke at his Iowa home in July 2022.

Since then, the American Pickers star has been on the mend, and now fans want to know how he's doing.

How is Frank Fritz doing?

Mike Wolfe put his beef with Frank aside after he suffered the stroke and asked fans to pray for his former co-star.

“I have been very private in the past year in regards to Frank's life and the journey he’s been on," he wrote in a social media post.

"There have been lots of opinions in regards to mine and Frank’s friendship and the show but now is not the time to set the record straight. Now is the time to pray for my friend.

"Frank has suffered a stroke and is in the hospital. Please keep him in your hearts and thoughts," he added.

Read More from Frank

At the beginning of August, Frank’s father, Bill Fritz, exclusively told The U.S. Sun that his son was in “stable” condition. 

Court papers obtained by The U.S. Sun show that Frank’s friend was appointed as his guardian, while a bank acts as a conservator to oversee his finances. 

The conservator will pay for Frank’s care and everyday living expenses, including health insurance, repairs, and tax on his property, and ensure he has appropriate transportation to medical appointments and activities.

The conservator will also ensure compliance with federal and state income tax and distribute funds for clothing and haircare.

The documents revealed just how debilitating the stroke was, as the papers read: “Because of his stroke, Mr. Fritz’s decision­-making capacity is so impaired that he is unable to care for his own safety, or to provide for necessities such as food, shelter, clothing, or medical care without which physical injury or illness may occur.

“Mr. Fritz’s decision­making capacity is so impaired that he is unable to make, communicate, or carry out important decisions concerning his own financial affairs.”

An exhibit was mentioned in the court papers from a doctor, confirming Frank “does not have decisional capacity.”

The legal papers continued: “Decisions must be made for Mr. Fritz’s care and placement while he continues to recover and receive treatment for his injuries. 

“Appointment of a guardian and conservator is necessary to avoid immediate harm to him.”

Since returning home from rehab, Fritz has had a new wheelchair ramp installed onto his Iowa farmhouse.

Why did Frank Fritz leave American Pickers?

Frank's stroke came one year after he was fired from his role at American Pickers.

He last appeared on the show during a March 2020 episode before he took time off to recover from a grueling back surgery that left him with 185 stitches and two rods in his spine.

Mike was the one who publicly revealed that Frank had been booted from the show in July 2021.

Although they were childhood friends, Frank exclusively revealed to The U.S. Sun that Mike has not reached out to him since he was fired from the show.

Frank said: “I haven’t talked to Mike in two years.

"He knew my back was messed up, but he didn’t call me up and ask how I was doing. That’s just how it is."

Since Frank was let go, The U.S. Sun has revealed that the show's ratings have plummeted to under one million viewers for the first time in years.

Viewers have claimed that it is "unwatchable" without Indiana native Frank. However, the ratings have since improved.

Season 24 of American Pickers premiered on January 4, 2023. Mike's brother Robbie Wolfe joined him as a co-host, in addition to "Jersey" Jon Szalay and Danielle Colby.

Fans can expect new episodes on The History Channel beginning July 5, 2023.

What is Frank Fritz's net worth?

Due to Frank's successful History Channel career, he has been able to amass a fortune.
