Im a chef - how to make the most mouthwatering mac and cheese in your Air Fryer

WITH chilly months ahead you may be craving some tasty comfort food to warm you up.

And now a chef has revealed exactly how to create the perfect mac and cheese in your Air Fryer as the perfect autumn and winter treat. 

TikTok user Chef Obang showed how she creates the mouth-watering dish in minutes. 

On her @chefobang account she showed how she first heats a pan and adds her onion, and then her ground beef and seasoning.

She then adds chopped sausage and her tomato pasta sauce.

Next she puts grated cheese in the pan so it can melt, and then oregano.

After adding a lid, she lets it simmer - before cooking her macaroni pasta separately. 

In a second frying pan, she melts two large knobs of butter and flour and milk to make a roux. 

Finally it’s time for the Air Fryer to come into its own.

In a foil tray, she adds her macaroni, followed by the tomato meat sauce, and then adds the roux on top.

Finally she adds grated cheese to the top and puts in her Air Fryer at 180 degrees for 10 minutes. 

The finished result looked incredible and thousands have liked the video.
