The 'sunset pastel' hair colour trend is the dreamiest way to go rainbow for spring

SUNSETS are usually the ultimate vision of romance and beauty - now it seems, you can transfer just that straight onto your hair.

The sunset inspired hairstyles are the latest beauty trend sweeping Instagram, and include some of the prettiest pastel shades.

We've seen rich auburn colours from the autumn, now its time to switch it up for spring.

Featuring soft purples, yellows and dusty pinks seamlessly blended together - the look really is like magic.

Most sunset hair posts have received plenty of admiring comments, so you should expect to see more and more people sporting the trend next season.

We may be suffering at the hands of a British snowstorm, but that doesn't mean we can't dream about these colours.

Now that days are getting lighter earlier (which means we aren't all waking up to darkness anymore) it might be the best time to think about a dramatic hair change.

Other people took inspiration from the deep orange and purple tones to create a more vibrant look.

So, if you fancy the brighter looking locks, this may be the one for you.

Here are some options if you want to try achieve the look at home...

 

Try a temporary home hair colour to to test out if the look could be for you

This pastel spray in shade cotton candy will help give you a better idea of what the sunset trend could do for you.

Trying a range of non-permanent colours that match the pastel colours in the trend is less damaging than experimenting with permanent.

The spray application is also a much less time consuming and hassle-free alternative.

If you're feeling brave, take the plunge and go for a permanent option instead.

The L'Oreal ombre effect comes in a shades similar to the rich oranges in the sunset trend, for those who prefer the more intense colours.
