Who is Fleabag star Phoebe Waller-Bridge dating?

PHOEBE Waller-Bridge is a British actress, writer and producer who created and starred in such hits as Fleabag.

Waller-Bridge has been in a relationship for the last few years following her divorce from Irish author and broadcaster Conor Woodman.

Who is Fleabag star Phoebe Waller-Bridge dating?

Waller-Bridge, a Golden Globe winner for her performance in Fleabag, has been linked to British playwright and filmmaker Martin McDonagh, an Oscar winner, over the last few years.

The couple keep their relationship private, but Waller-Bridge mentioned in an interview that she saw McDonagh's play The Pillowman before they met - and was very impressed.

“It was the first play I saw that made me think theatre can be really exciting," she told The Guardian in 2018.

"Sometimes you need to see something like that - a piece of work that wakes you up, that makes you go, ‘Yes, of course that can be done’ and gives your imagination permission to go 17 million other places. I felt like that about his work.”

When asked by The Guardian if this the happiest she has been in a relationship, she responded: “I’m not going to talk about that, either.”

She had previously declined to discuss her marriage to Woodman.

McDonagh won an Academy Award for Best Live Action Short Film in 2006 for his film Six Shooter.

He would later received two Oscar nominations for his work on 2017's Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri, including for Best Director.

When will Waller-Bridge co-star with Harrison Ford?

Waller-Bridge has been cast in the fifth installment of the Indiana Jones franchise, according to Deadline.
