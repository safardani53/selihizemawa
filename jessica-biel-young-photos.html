Photos From Her Young Days To Today Hollywood Life

Oh, isn’t she a gem! Jessica Biel, born March 3, 1982 in Ely, Minnesota, is a true blue All-American girl. She started singing her heart out in musicals but found stardom at the age of 14 playing the rebellious eldest daughter, Mary Camden, on 7th Heaven, pictured on the show in 1996 here.

After growing up on the small screen, Jessica took the world of cinema by storm with her bone-chilling performance in the horror flick, The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (2003). She continued to wow audiences with her versatility in films like The Rules of Attraction (2002), Blade: Trinity (2004), The Illusionist (2006), I Now Pronounce You Chuck & Larry (2007), Valentine’s Day (2010), The A-Team (2010), New Year’s Eve (2011), Total Recall (2012), and Hitchcock (2012).

Jessica made a grand return to television in 2017, leading and executive producing the limited series, The Sinner. Her spine-tingling portrayal earned her nominations for a Golden Globe Award and a Primetime Emmy Award. She’d continued conquering TV with her standout performance in the 2022 true-crime drama Candy.

And did we mention that Jessica is a pro at balancing work and family? She and the charming Justin Timberlake tied the knot in 2012 and have two adorable children together.
