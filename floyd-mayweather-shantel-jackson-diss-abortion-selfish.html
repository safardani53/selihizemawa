Floyd Mayweather’s Shantel Jackson Diss: ‘She’s Selfish’ About Looks

Floyd opened up about his decision to go public with Chantel’s sonogram in a new radio interview with V-103, TMZ reports.

He clearly has no regrets about his Facebook post because all he did in the interview was slam Chantel even more, claiming that her vanity is what motivated her to get an abortion.

“It hurt my feelings, all because she’s selfish because all she’s worried about is looks, talking about ‘I don’t want to mess my body up.’ Everything you got on your body, I paid for so it’s okay. Even if you had the baby — even if you had the babies, if you wanted me to put you back together like I did in the beginning, I could’ve done it again,” he said.

Floyd went on to diss Shantel’s appearance and alleged extensive plastic surgery, saying, “I’m all like, ‘Everything you got on you is fake. You know you got a fake butt, you’ve got fake titties, you got work done on your face. That’s how you know my love was genuine because I accepted you from the heart: no breasts, no butt, no face work. I accepted you because it was real love. Pure love.'”

Floyd sure is laying the disses on thick — and we can’t help but think that that’s no way to treat someone you once loved so “purely!”

Floyd Mayweather Blasts Ex-Fiancee For Aborting Their Twin Babies

On May 1, Floyd shared a picture of Chantel’s alleged sonogram on his official Facebook page, alongside the caption:

The real reason me and Shantel Christine Jackson @missjackson broke up was because she got a abortion, and I’m totally against killing babies. She killed our twin babies. #ShantelJackson #FloydMayweather #TheMoneyTeam #TMT

The boxer has since deleted the Facebook post. Floyd and Shantel, who dated for four years, broke off their engagement more than a year ago.
