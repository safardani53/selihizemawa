Superdrug shoppers nabbing half-price perfumes 'perfect for Xmas presents' - and make-up bags are sc

SUPERDRUG shoppers are racing to buy half-price perfumes and make-up bags slashed to 45p.

The health and beauty retailer has slashed the price of some designer fragrances by half-price.

Some scents have even more than 50% off. 

One shopper alerted people to the hefty discounts on Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK.

She called the perfumes an “absolute bargain”, while others were quick to note that the fancy bottles would be "perfect for Christmas presents".

However, you have to be a Superdrug member to nab the discounts. If you’re not, you can easily sign up online or in-store.

Among the perfumes included are Ghost Deep Night Eau De Toilette, with a 75ml bottle being dropped to £25 from £50.

A 50ml bottle of BOSS Femme For Her Eau de Parfum now costs £30 rather than £57 with the membership. 

Ghost The Fragrance 150ml Eau de Toilette Spray is even cheaper than half-price for members, costing £32 for 150ml rather than £70. 

As is the Versace Versus Red Jeans Eau de Toilette Spray, which now costs £15 instead of £32.

A 75ml bottle of BOSS Orange Woman Eau de Toilette is £37 down from £70 for members.

Another shopper picked up a 45p make-up bag from the retailer. 

The sequin rainbow zip case, by Superdrug’s Studio London brand, was slashed to the penny price in store as part of a clearance sale.

To become a Superdrug member, you need to pick up a Health & Beauty Card.

You can sign up and order online, or alternatively get one at the till on your next trip.

The retailer gives members exclusive lower prices for selected products.

You can also earn points and use them as part-payment every time you shop.

This means that you don’t have to wait too long before you start seeing the savings.
