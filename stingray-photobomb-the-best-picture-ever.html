Stingray Photobomb: The Best Picture Ever?

A group of three women had just finished snorkeling in the Cayman Islands when a giant stingray felt compelled to produce perhaps the greatest photobomb of all time.

Terrifying for them. Hilarious for us.

The fish likely had some help. The photobomb, posted on Reddit this week, appears to show a man in the water behind the women lifting the stingray up at the absolute perfect moment.

Nicely played by both.

 

Several commenters say the image originated from Stingray City, a popular Caymans tourist destination where swimmers and snorkelers are encouraged to feed the sea creatures.

“Come kiss a stingray with us this year!” boat charter service StingrayCityTrips (dot) com recommends.

“Standing in only three feet of water you will be surrounded by more than two dozen friendly stingrays.”

Some of whom may want to pose for photos with you. SMILE!
