General Mills introduces limited-edition cereal for $3 and customers will love the seasonal flavor

GENERAL Mills has introduced a limited-edition cereal for the fall.

Customers can find a box now starting at just $3.

Pumpkin spice Cheerios are back, manufacturer General Mills has confirmed.

Available for a limited time only, this seasonal edition adds that familiar fall flavor to the classic breakfast cereal.

Pumpkin spice Cheerios are made with real pumpkin puree and a blend of cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg.

Two sized boxes are available:

  • Mid-size - $3.00
  • Family size - $4.50

Customers can find the seasonal variety at select retailers across the US.

Not only can the gluten-free cereal be enjoyed with milk, but also dry as a snack, or as an ingredient for trail mix.

General Mills is just one of many popular brands now getting in on pumpkin spice season.

On your next trip to the grocery store, you may also come across a huge range of at-home coffee products from Starbucks with a pumpkin spice theme.

Shoppers can choose from an assortment of creamers, cold brews, and ground coffees with this festive flavor.

Starbucks says the new products have been created for those "waiting for Starbucks’ fall handcrafted beverages to return to cafés."

The coffee house has not yet confirmed when its famous pumpkin spice latte will be available again.

But a leaked menu circulating online has indicated that it will return on August 24, along with two new limited-time beverages:

  • Iced apple crisp oat milk shaken espresso
  • Iced pumpkin cream chai tea latte

Meanwhile, Krispy Kreme has already unveiled its full fall menu, including two brand-new donuts.

Customers can look out for a pumpkin spice variety box in grocery stores too.
