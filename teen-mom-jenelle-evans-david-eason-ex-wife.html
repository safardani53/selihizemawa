SNEAKY TEXTS

David was caught sending messages to his ex Whitney Johnson earlier this month.

Verified text messages sent from David's cell phone to Whitney on May 5, 2022, also show the former Teen Mom 2 star ruthlessly slamming his wife Jenelle.

Whitney is David's ex-wife who he married in 2006. His first child, Maryssa Rose, was born to her in August of 2007.

The two had a messy divorce which resulted in Eason gaining full custody of daughter Maryssa in 2017.

Jenelle's husband began the conversations with his ex that afternoon to discuss who was to pick up their daughter Maryssa, 16. 

The discussion, however, quickly veered into territory that his ex Whitney has called "inappropriate."

David, frustrated with Jenelle, first shared a screen grab of his conversations with his wife, where the Teen Mom star asks David: "Are u going or me to get Kaiser?" to which David replies: "Maryssa doesn't get picked up till 5:30 today."

After he shared a screen grab of his exchange with Jenelle with his ex-wife, he told her: "Now this, I can never win."

"She's mad because I have two loads worth of laundry. She has the entire room covered with her clothes," he wrote to Whitney and then shared a photo from inside the bedroom he shares with Jenelle in their home in North Carolina. 

The photo shows Jenelle and David's bedroom with clothes nearly covering the entire floor as well as piled on their bed.

There is also trash littered about and debris scattered atop their dresser. 

"Literally, this is all her s**t. Not one article of this clothing is mine but I'm a POS for cleaning.

"Sorry, I just need someone to vent to I have no friends except JD and he runs his mouth when he gets drunk. As if cleaning my own mess is worse than cheating. LMAO," David continued to rant.

Whitney then pivots the conversation back to the issue of who is going to pick up their daughter, as was his reasoning for reaching out in the first place. 

Speaking exclusively to The U.S. Sun, Whitney called the exchange "inappropriate," while her husband Shane Rich revealed he was sitting next to her throughout the day as the texts took place.

Whitney said: "A bedroom is a couple's private space, that's their bedroom, their intimate place - it was crazy that he would send me photos of it."

Shane said: "I was sitting right next to her. She was showing me the texts like, 'Can you believe this?' and I was telling her to keep responding to see where David was going with all of this."

"And I dream about you all the time. I don't know how I ended up here instead of where I wanted to be. I don't know how WE ended up here instead of where I wanted US to be."
