I Love Them So Much!

Iggy Azalea may have fake breasts. But she has some real pride over them.

The artist, who admitted earlier this month that she got a boob job in late 2014, was asked about the new body additions at the iHeartRadio Music Awards last night.

And she didn’t show any shame whatsoever over them while chatting with reporters.

"I just love them so much, I had to talk about them," Azalea said prior to taking the stage and performing her hit "Trouble" alongside Jennifer Hudson.

Friends of the singer apparently advised Azalea to let the chest enhancement speak for itself, but the Australian star decided to take the honest approach.

Mostly because she couldn’t help it.

"Everybody did say, ‘I don’t think you need to say it because nobody will ever know because they’re so small!’ " she joked, adding:

"I said, ‘I know, but I’m just gonna say it because I have a blabber mouth.’"

Azalea first opened up about the cosmetic change in the April issue of Vogue, telling the publication that she "thought about it my entire life."

She’s denied getting butt implants, but says the pressure of looking a certain way in Hollywood contributed to the boob job decision.

"When I first got to the States, people told me I should think about modeling,” Azalea tol Vogue. “So I went to a few agencies, but once they measured my body, they didn’t like me anymore.” 

That’s a sad story. But, hey, happy ending: it led to some huge breasts!
