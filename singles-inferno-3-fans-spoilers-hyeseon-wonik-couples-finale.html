Singles Inferno 3 fans spot major clues revealing endgame couple weeks ahead of finale epis

SINGLE’S Inferno fans think they’ve figured out one of the endgame couples for Season 3 after noticing a few major clues.

The Netflix reality show isn’t set to release its season finale for another few weeks, but one of the cast members seemed to drop a huge spoiler.

With five of its 10 episodes now streaming, Single’s Inferno 3 is still only halfway done, but that hasn’t stopped fans from speculating about how it will all end.

Episodes 6 and 7 will drop on December 26, the next two episodes on January 2, and the big finale on January 9.

Now that the show is in the middle of its season, many of the cast members have been active on social media and feeding their new followers plenty of content.

Fans think star Son Won-ik may have just accidentally given out a major clue as to who he ends up with on the show, though.

He recently spent some quality time with Yu Si-eun in Paradise, where he revealed he’s a realtor for premium properties, but many fans don’t envision the two as a long-term pair.

Instead, viewers believe he and Choi Hye-seon will leave the show as a couple, and they think Won-ik has just given them a clue on Instagram.

Shortly after the new episodes of Single’s Inferno 3 dropped on Netflix on Tuesday, the contestants joined together for a party to celebrate the show.

Won-ik shared a sweet video from the event with many of his co-stars, but followers noticed he seemed to include an extra special moment with Hye-seon.

The video began with the real estate broker and a few of the other cast members all smiling at the camera, making funny faces, and waving.

Won-ik then shared a clip of some of the stars at a table enjoying drinks, treats, and each other’s company.

The room was decked out in pink, white, and red heart-shaped balloons to go with the dating and love themes of the reality show.

Then it was time for Won-ik to stop with the group shots and focus in on just one person: Hye-seon.

He filmed her as she showed off her now-long hair – after rocking a more cropped look on the show – and her fit figure in a body-hugging black dress.

As Won-ik recorded her, she was busy holding up her phone and recording him right back.

He then hopped into the view of his camera, so both he and Hye-seon were in the frame.

They both smiled and laughed, before taking turns to fully look at one another.

In the background, a remix version of the song Agora Hills continued to play, but fans insisted the lyrics, “I wanna show you off,” took on a whole new meaning during this portion.

The video wrapped up with six of the stars all waving to the camera, with Won-ik front and center, and Hye-seon right next to him.

Fans flooded the comments section to gush over how the two look “really good together” and that he seemed to be dropping clues. 

One Instagram user theorized: “I think hyeseon and wonik endgame.”

A second agreed, writing: “I guess they'll leave the inferno together”

A third pointed out: “The song.. ‘I wanna show you off’ and he literally included a clip of hyeseon and him… IS IT A HINT THO?!

“I’m shipping these two so hard rn”

Other fans noticed Won-ik often reposted Instagram Stories featuring fan edits of him and Hyeseon and speculated this was because they ended up together.

“Living off the wonik hyeseon crumbs,” one fan wrote after reading about some of the clues fans pointed out.

A second commented: “I’m the captain of the Woonik x Hyeseon ship”

Fans will have to wait until the Single's Inferno 3 finale releases on Netflix on January 9 to find out if their theory is correct.
