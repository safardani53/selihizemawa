Candice Swanepoel Biography

At age of 15, Candice Swanepoel grabbed the attention of a model scout in a Durban Flea market. Later, with her endeavor and talent, she got herself on the cover of Spanish, Australian, Brazilian, Portuguese, Japanese, Italian, Hellenic and many other. Likewise, Candice has walked the runway for several agencies including Chanel, Tommy Hilfiger, Fendi, Ralph, Christian Dior, Rag & Bone and Givenchy.

Candice made her appearance in various advertisement campaigns such as Miu Miu, Tommy Hilfiger, Rag & Bone, Ralph Lauren, Nike, Juicy Countre as well as for Victoria’s Secret. She was featured as a model in the 2010 “SWIM” catalog alongside Behati Prinsloo, Erin Heatherton, Lindsay Ellingson, and Rosie Huntington-Whiteley.

Model Swanepoel is of white ethnicity and holds African nationality. Currently, she is unmarried but involved in a romantic love affair with her boyfriend, Hermann Nicoli who is a Brazilian male model. They first met in Paris when Candice was 17. Now the couple is engaged and blessed with a son, Anaca.

Swanepoel has been ranked as a top 10 earning model by Forbes. As of 2017, she has a net worth of 25 million dollars through which she is living a celebrity life.
