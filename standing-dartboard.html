7 Top Rated Standing Dartboard

Dart games have always been a traditional pub game, and you can find them at almost every arcade game center. If you want to add a fun experience to your home, then standing dartboards will be an ideal choice for you. 

The standing dartboards require a space at your home. However, once you make the purchase, you will get the experience in your living space, free from the crowd and noises.

There are various kinds of models you can get in the market. There are traditional ones made of wood, and then there are electronic ones with modern features. As a result, it might get confusing when you make a purchase.

In this article, we bring you some of the top-rated standing dartboards of 2024. Please stick with us until the end of the article.

Top Rated Standing Dartboard

7. SPRAWL Dartboard Standing Dart Board Set

  • L x W x H: Not Specified

We are starting our list with the SPRAWL standing dartboard set. This set is ideal if you are looking for something small and traditional design for tournament-style play.

The SPRAWL standing dartboard set has a practical space-saving design and is ideal for small spaces. Similarly, the double-sided flocking board is made of super-dense and highly-visible flocking.

The set is made of a durable metal frame, and the stand is made of non-toxic materials powder coated for durability. The metal of the bracket is made of firm iron; thus, it is not easily deformed.

Likewise, the set has cool-looking spider wiring made from galvanized steel. The galvanized steel further adds to the high tensile strength and adds its durability.

The most incredible feature of the set is its smooth-rolling mobile dartboard stand. The 360 swivel quality wheels enable the dartboard’s movement to stand easily and safely. The four coasters have brakes on them for locking the stands.

SPRAWL dartboard set is an excellent choice if you have kids, as the height of the stand is adjustable. The set has one dartboard, one L-shaped stand, an arm stand, support arm stands, six darts, all hardware, and instructions.

6. CenterPoint Solid Wood Dartboard Cabinet

  • L x W x H: 9.25 x 24 x 81 inches

This dartboard cabinet by Hathaway is ideal if you want something that can upgrade your game room. The CenterPoint solid wood dartboard cabinet will provide arcade-style fun at home.

The dartboard cabinet has a solid poplar with a dark cherry finish. It has the 15.5 inches regulation size that can keep scores for up to eight players. Thus, you can easily entertain your friends and family.

Similarly, the additional feature of the set is its 29 games with over 90 variations. As a result, there is plenty of options you can choose from. The voice and sound system provides additional entertainment to the gameplay.

Sturdy base stabilizers support the CenterPoint solid wood dartboard cabinet. The free-standing leg supports the cabinet upright, and the wall mounts help to add further stability.

The standing dartboard is versatile and convenient, along with an excellent cherry finish that can add to your game room aesthetic. You can use the cabinet, sturdy storage shelves, and integrated dart holders to store away the accessories after the gameplay.

The dartboard cabinet comes complete with six darts and six spare tips.

5. Arachnid E650 Free Standing Dartboard Cabinet Set

  • L x W x H: 20.4 x 11 x 82.6 inches

Our 5th pick is the Arachnid E650 free-standing dartboard cabinet set. The set has a rustic tan wood grain finish that will add to your home décor while giving you the arcade experience.

Arachnid E650 free-standing dartboard cabinet set features 24 games and 132 variations. The cabinet has LED lighting to enhance the gaming experience further.

Similarly, the dartboard has the 15.5 inches regulation target area. It also features a tournament-style spider and colors. The weight of the cabinet is 60 pounds which makes it the lightest standing dartboard on our list.

One of the unique features of the set is its micro-thin segment dividers. This feature helps to reduce bounce-outs dramatically. Likewise, the Nylon Tough segments help to improve playability and durability.

The Arachnid E650 free-standing dartboard cabinet set comes with six soft-tip darts and has a dartboard cabinet. You can neatly tuck away the accessories after a great game of darts.

4. Spider 360 Galaxy 3 Home Edition Dartboard – YRB

  • L x W x H: Not Specified

If you are looking for a standing dartboard with a modern, sleek look, then we recommend this next dartboard. The Spider 360 Galaxy 3 Home Edition dartboard – YRB is the ideal electronic dart machine that will check all your requirement.

Apart from its sleek design, the dartboard’s first noticeable feature is its tri-color LED illumination. It gives the dartboard very eye-catching visual effects and also eliminates all shadows.

Similarly, the dartboard has a 19-inch wide LCD flat screen monitor and a 15-inch regulation size target. It has multiple game options with auto or manual player options. Likewise, it also has a single or double bull option.

Another great feature of the Spider 360 Galaxy 3 Home Edition dartboard – YRB is its durable, impact-resistant cabinet. After you finish your game, you can put away the accessories neatly in the cabinet.

This standing dartboard is one of the top-rated products and is worth every penny. It comes with six darts and 500 pieces of .25-inch soft plastic tips. 

3. Shelti Eye 2 Electronic Dart Board Machine

  • L x W x H: 24.25 x 24 x 86 inches

The Shelti Eye 2 Electronic dartboard machine is one of the best-standing dartboards on the market that can be easily folded in half if you want to transport it. Because of this unique feature, it is our third pick for the top-rated standing dartboard.

The dartboard has a plywood cabinet design, making it easy to set up, break down, and transport. It has standardized and adjustable handicap systems compliant with leading dart associations.

A notable feature of the machine is its Dot matrix display area. This provides instruction to the players before and during the gameplay. Likewise, it also improves communication of feats afterward.

The display helps personalize the game to the operator, location, and player. Shelti Eye 2 Electronic dartboard machine also includes the National Dart Association guidelines for sports points and handicapping sports marks.

The machine has a backlit LCD and a 3-button menu to enhance setup and statistics reporting. Likewise, the TRU-SCORE feature helps with final scoring and accuracy. It includes many old classic games and speed-based games for fun hours.

Shelti Eye 2 Electronic dartboard machine has multiple games and game options with auto and manual player changes. It also includes delay timers for automatic and manual player changes. It is definitely worth your investment.

2. Arachnid Cricket Pro 800 Standing Electronic Dartboard

  • L x W x H: 51.75 x 26.5 x 5.5 inches

For number 2, we have Arachnid Cricket Pro 800 standing electronic dartboard. It has an arcade-style look with a beautiful mahogany finish which makes a perfect piece for your living space.

This standing electronic dartboard is the top product of the Arachnid electronic dartboard line. Aside from its great look, the dartboard has a furniture-quality free-standing cabinet with routed doors and a storage compartment.

Similarly, it features the Arachnid Pro 800 tournament soft tip dartboard with a regulation 15.5 inches target area. It has an eight-player score display with four scores shown simultaneously.

The Arachnid Cricket Pro 800 standing electronic dartboard also features 39 games with 179 options, including seven cricket games. Likewise, it has an optional three-level heckler and mounting hardware.

However, the standing dartboard will require assembly and includes dartboard accessories.

1. Spider 360 2000 Series Home Commercial Grade Dart Board

  • L x W x H: 18.5 x 27 x 79.5 inches

Our number 1 pick is the Spider 360 2000 Series Home Commercial Grade dartboard. The brand boasts this product as the dartboard of champions and is worth investing in.

It has an all-new leaderboard, an online global ranking system based on the player’s skill level. It tracks the player’s marks per round or points per dart.

As a result, the players can test their skills and get ranked by seeing their names displayed on all Spider Series dart games and the mobile app. Similarly, the dartboard has a built-in computer known as Mark Dart.

Players can play against the built-in Mark at four levels, one amateur to four masters. It can adjust to the player’s style of play or even teach them to play darts better. Up to three players can play at a time.

The Spider 360 2000 Series Home Commercial Grade dartboard has over 40 game options. It has a large 19-inch LCD flat screen LCD monitor with a 15-inch target. 

There is an option for auto or manual players and a single or double bull option. The brand has reinvented electronic scoring to increase engagement focus and entertainment. It comes with six darts and 500 pieces of 0.25 soft plastic tips.

Conclusion

The commercial-grade models are a bit on the hefty price line, but they are made of superior quality and built to last. But some home-use models are cheaper but still provide a fantastic gaming experience.

So make sure you decide whether you want commercial or home-use models and consider your budget. But whichever your choice is, these standing dartboards will undoubtedly give you an excellent game experience in the comfort of your home!
