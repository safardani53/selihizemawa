What happened to Deadliest Catch's Todd Kochutin?

TODD Kochutin was one of the stars of the long-running series Deadliest Catch.

He became the latest Deadliest Catch member to die after passing away on February 26, 2021, at the age of 30.

What happened to Deadliest Catch's Todd Kochutin?

Deadliest Catch fans were shocked to learn of Kochutin's death in April 2022, seeing as he had died over a year prior.

At this time, details surrounding the circumstances of his death are still vague, but we know it came after he sustained injuries while on board the F/V Patricia Lee.

In footage that was released by Discovery UK, fans were able to get an idea of what happened to the television personality.

In the clip, crew members learned that one of their own needed medical attention, and Captain Sig Hansen later revealed that someone might have been crushed by a crab pot, which can weigh up to 800 pounds, according to PEOPLE.
