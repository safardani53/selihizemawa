Aron Piper Career

Aron Piper began his career as an actor, he made his acting debut as an extra in the Gunman in 2004. He was also featured in Maktub (2011) as an Inaki.

Subsequently, he starred as Ruben Santos and Andrian Muriana in two episodes of the medical drama series Centro Medico (2016 and 2017). In 2018, he portrayed Chico in the short film Un Minuto.

Aron Piper was cast in the pivotal role of Ander Munoz in Carlos Montero and Dario Madrona’s Spanish-language teen thriller drama Elite on Netflix in 2018. The show received two and three-season renewals in 2019–2020 due to its immense popularity.
