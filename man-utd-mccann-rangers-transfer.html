Man Utd academy prospect Charlie McCann joins Rangers B on permanent transfer after being scouted by

MANCHESTER UNITED academy midfielder Charlie McCann has officially completed a permanent transfer to Rangers.

The 19-year-old has never made his senior debut for the Red Devils but was a regular in their under-23 side before sealing a switch to Scotland.

The Manchester Evening News claim the deal is reportedly worth around £750,000 and will see the player link up with Rangers' B team first.

Speaking to Rangers' official website, McCann admitted he made the decision to leave Old Trafford in search of first-team football.

He said: "I’m delighted to get the move over the line, I was obviously really excited when I heard the news and I knew that it was a no brainer.

"I can’t wait to get started now and I’m looking forward to it.
