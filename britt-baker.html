Britt Baker | Early Life

Baker (birth name Brittany Baker) was born on April 23, 1991, under the star sign Taurus in Punxsutawney, Pennsylvania, the U.S.

She was born to her parents, Mary Ann Baker and Sam Baker. She is the oldest of two children and has a younger brother named Dane Baker. 

Education

Regarding her educational background, Britt Baker earned a degree from Pennsylvania State University in behavioral medicine with a minor in human development and family studies. 

Following it, she enrolled at the University of Pittsburgh School of Dental Medicine. In May 2018, she graduated in dentistry with distinction. 

Britt Baker | Career

Baker had been training for wrestling alongside her studies.

In 2014, she joined the International Wrestling Cartel Training Academy in South Hills, Pennsylvania. She trained under Super Hentai and Marshall Gambino back then. 

While studying dentistry, she attended Absolute Intense Wrestling in Cleveland, Ohio. 

The Professional Arena

In 2015, Britt Baker made her professional career debut at the International Wrestling Cartel (I.W.C.) event.

Similarly, in 2016, she appeared in WWE Raw, and by the end of the year, she had become the inaugural I.W.C. Women’s Champion.

Later in 2019, Britt Baker joined the All Elite Wrestling (A.E.W.) and made her debut in May. Soon after that, she appeared in the Dynamite series.

Not long after she took part in the A.E.W. Women’s World Championship Eliminator Tournament, she bagged the A.E.W. Women’s World Championship.

Britt’s Two Different Lines Of Profession

In addition to being a professional wrestler, Britt is also a dentist. 

She works in her dental office on Mondays and Tuesdays. Often on Tuesdays, she works late hours, until around 4 or 5 pm.

She then goes on to play for Dynamite airs on Wednesdays. She returns to her work at the dental office whenever she has no wrestling match. 

Most of her weekends are spent on appearances and signings. She spends some of her weekends sitting at home and doing laundry. 

“Many people don’t want to work a 9-to-5 job, but for me, that 9-to-5 job keeps me grounded and in a good headspace. It also keeps me humble because I go from being a rock star on T.V. to being in the dental office. They don’t care who I am in the ring. So I appreciate it on that scale.”
– Britt Baker

Injuries

To date, Britt Baker has sustained several injuries in her professional Career.

In 2019, she sustained a concussion during the A.E.W. Fight for the Fallen in a tag team match in Jacksonville. The concussion occurred when she faced a kick by Priestley during the match. 

Later in 2020, Britt suffered a leg injury during a tag team match. The injury occurred when Statlander and Shida threw Nyla Rose during the match, and she accidentally landed on Baker’s knee. 

The same year in July, Britt Baker underwent surgery to fix her broken nose and deviated septum.

Britt Baker | Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.s)

What is Britt Baker’s wrestling persona like? 

Most of the time, Britt Baker’s profession as a dentist is referred to in her games, and it later becomes a parody.

Initially, they described her character as “happy” but later stood out as “too proud and delusional.”

Then, her characters gradually named “Dr.” and “D.M.D.” to enhance “her character’s obnoxiousness.”

What Are Britt Baker’s Wrestling moves?

Baker uses a fusion of a scissored armbar and the mandible claw as her finisher. This move is named the Lockjaw and is to attack the opponent’s mouth.
