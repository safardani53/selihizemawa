I jetted to Turkey for fox eye surgery - I love the snatched look but trolls say it looks terrifying

AN AESTHETIC practitioner has been savaged after revealing she jetted to Turkey for fox eye surgery.

Nasrin went under the knife and documented the before and after results on TikTok - but it seemed not everyone was impressed.

The 37-year-old who lives in the UK flew to Turkey for the op, and shared before and after pictures of her surgical transformation a month on in a short slip.

The beauty fan said: "So this is before the most recent surgery, and then this is about three weeks afterward.

"It is still very swollen, very bruised and obviously, very, very lifted.

"This is not the final result, the results are still coming and the area is still healing."

In another clip, Nasrin shared the realities of what it's like getting multiple surgeries done at once.

She revealed she didn't just get fox eye surgery while in Turkey.

Nasrin said: "I just wanted to show you this because this is a reality when you get multiple surgeries - this is what it looks like.''

In the now-viral video uploaded on TikTok, the beauty enthusiast, could be seen with severe bruising and swelling around the eye area, as well as having trouble speaking.

''No one ever shows this. Like, I'm in agony,'' said Nasrin who was under anaesthesia for close to a whopping eight hours.

While Nasrin wants to document the realities of surgery, many still savaged her on social media.

Her latest video posted to her account @aestheticcliniclondon soon went viral with over 60k views and over 700 likes.

People were quick to take to the comments to share their thoughts.

One person wrote: "That’s gonna look crazy after a couple years."

Another commented: "Looks scary."

"Why would you do it to yourself," penned a third.

Meanwhile a fourth said: "It looks horrible."

"Omg before was so much better girl! I’m just being honest with you! I'm also a plastic surgery-addicted person but I’m telling you before was so fine,” claimed a fifth.
