How do I watch Quest TV and what time are Carabao Cup semi-final highlights on for free TONIGHT?

THE Carabao Cup is breaking new ground with highlights switching channel this season.

Here's all fans of Chelsea and Tottenham need to know to watch their entertaining semi-final back tonight.

What TV channel are Carabao Cup highlights on?

Carabao Cup highlights are now live on Quest TV - a channel which also shows the EFL highlights.

What time are Carabao Cup highlights on?

Carabao Cup second leg highlights will be shown at 10.30pm on Thursday, January 24.

How do I watch Quest TV for FREE?

Quest is available for FREE on terrestrial TV through Freeview channel 37.

Sky subscribers should tune in to channel 144, while users of Virgin will find Quest on channel 172.

The Discovery-owned channel normally shows lifestyle programmes but has shown pre-season tournaments before.

In 2014, Quest showed the Schalke 04 Cup, which featured Newcastle, West Ham, Schalke and Malaga.

And in 2017 it secured rights to show Arsenal's Emirates Cup matches live.

And in the 2018/19 season it will show EFL highlights.
