Love and Marriage Huntsville reunion should bring up partners receipts as drama continues

The Love and Marriage Huntsville reunion is on its way, as fans scramble to find out the episode’s date and time. LAMH drama has seen it all this season, from Mel and Martell’s relationship quarrels to Maurice and Kimmi’s lack of intimacy. The season 6 reunion will lay all of the issues bare.

OWN’s reality TV series, Love and Marriage Huntsville, will see Carlos King host part 1 of the reunion. LaTisha and Stormi are expected to trade jabs over business practices, while the gang discusses Kimmi’s lack of intimacy and Maurice’s lack of tact. The TV stars also confront KeKe about those wild accusations.

Love and Marriage Huntsville reunion time

The Love and Marriage Huntsville reunion part 1 time is on Saturday, January 6, at 8/7C. Part 2 airs a week later, on Saturday, January 13, and will be hosted by Carlos King, at the same time.

Part two will see LaTisha and KeKe trade accusations of infidelity, Carlos confront Martell about his dating life and lingering feelings for Melody, and Tiffany and Lou discuss their future on the show.

Stormi’s mother confronts Melody in part two. Following the wild drama between Martell and Melody on the season finale, LAMH fans hope she “brings up Martell’s past” during the reunion.

LAMH reunion – ‘Bring up receipts’

During the December 30 episode of LAMH, the final one before the big reunion, Martell extended an olive branch to Melody, but she was having absolutely none of it after his infidelity.

One fan wrote: “Agent of chaos is the perfect description for Martell #LAMH.” Another penned: “Martell want Mel back so badly. I pray Mel sours HIGH without him. #LAMH.”

“I said after the last episode that Martell never expected that Mel would walk away. Tonight confirmed that. He thought he could do what/whoever and Mel would stay with him,” said a viewer.

Marsau and Tisha’s relationship predictions

Marsau and Tisha’s marriage took a back step compared to the other LAMH relationships this season, but fans have predictions about how things will go for the couple at the reunion.

The couple have been married for 12 years, and claim to have never stepped out on each other. Marsau proudly has the words, ‘Wife of Marsau’ in her Instagram bio, so they are definitely still together.

A fan said, “I don’t want to see another marriage fall apart. But I hope Tisha standing on business gets Marsau to respect his wife and her feelings now that the tables have turned. #LAMH.”
