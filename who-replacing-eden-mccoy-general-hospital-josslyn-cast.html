Who is temporarily replacing Eden McCoy on General Hospital?

EDEN McCoy was first introduced to General Hospital in October 2015 as Josslyn Jax.

Now news broke that McCoy will be temporarily taking a break from the soap — here's everything we know about the actress who'll be replacing her.

Who is temporarily replacing Eden McCoy on General Hospital?

On Monday, December 18, 2023, fans tuned in to General Hospital to see an unfamiliar face playing the role of Josslyn Jax.

Actress Courtney Fulk took on the role as Soap Opera News revealed McCoy would be taking a temporary break from the show.

There are no details around McCoy's absence but many believe it has to do with the actress losing her mom to cancer in November 2023.

McCoy shared the news on Instagram saying: "My mother has fought an intense battle with cancer for the past two and a half years and has now left us physically.

At this time, there's no word as to how long Fulk will be covering for McCoy.

Who is Courtney Fulk?

Courtney Fulk is an up-and-coming American actress from Los Angeles, California.

Fulk began her acting career in 2021 when she appeared in the TV film, Killer Cheer Mom.

She went on to appear in the 2022 TV film Student Seduction.

Her role as Josslyn Jacks is not only her biggest role to date but also her first breakout role on daytime TV.

How can I watch General Hospital?

Fans can catch Courtney Fulk's debut as Josslyn Jacks on weekdays on ABC at 3 pm ET.

Additionally, General Hospital fans can rewatch previous seasons on streaming services such as Hulu, fuboTV, and SlingTV.

General Hospital made its debut on primetime TV in 1963.
