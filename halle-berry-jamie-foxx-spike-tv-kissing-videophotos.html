Halle Berry &amp; Jamie Foxx Spike TV KISSING! (VIDEO,PHOTOS)

Halle Berry was the recipient of the Decades of Hotness Award last night at the Spike TV 2009 Guys Choice Awards at Sony Studios in Los Angeles.

Presenting the 42-year-old actress with her award was Jamie Foxx. He gave her a chaste kiss on the cheek and she payed back the favor in spades.

The third annual event paid tribute to the things guys love most. The Brass Balls Award went to Clint Eastwood for his lifetime of legendary work as an actor and filmmaker. The Guitar God Award went to Eddie Van Halen. The top honor of the evening, Guy of the Year, went to Mickey Rourke. See the full list of winners here.

The taped show will be broadcast by Spike TV on Sunday, June 21 at 10:00 p.m. ET/PT.
