Move Up From the Sippy Cup!

When your child has outgrown the sippy, but isn’t exactly ready for a real glass — the Siliskin Glass ($7.95) is the perfect in between cup. Created by Silikids, it’s a six-ounce glass covered with a grippy, protective silicone sleeve that peels right off. My 4-year-old son tested it out and after more than a few drops, it was still intact.

The cup is also easy to clean. Just throw it in the dishwasher. It’s also 100% safe — both the glass and silicone are BPA-free, plus the silicone is anti-fungal, hypo-allergenic and recyclable. Pick aqua or lime (left) — either way your little one will love how easy it is to grip and hold onto.
