Who Is Josh Radnors Girlfriend Now? A Look at His Dating History

Actor Josh Radnor famous for playing Ted Mosby on the Emmy Award-winning CBS sitcom How I Met Your Mother, is currently rumored to be dating Abigail Spencer.

According to Daily Mail, Radnor was spotted spending quality time with the actress earlier in 2021.

Is Abigail Spencer Josh Radnor's Girlfriend?

In January 2021, Radnor was pictured going for a walk with his rumored girlfriend and former co-star in Montecito, California.

Radnor was photographed alongside Spencer, holding his furry brown dog as they walked to a nearby park.

Spencer was seen wearing a pink turtleneck sweater with a floral print skirt and fur-lined Ugg boots in the images.

At the same time, Radnor donned a rather casual outfit comprising a graphic print t-shirt underneath a blue flannel button-up with cuffed blue jeans and burgundy sneakers.

Additionally, both were seen with face masks for obvious reasons that they took off later when they shared a meal with their friends.

Around the same time, Spencer was noticed interacting with Radnor on Twitter.

However, speculations of the actors potentially dating each other began in 2020.

On May 22 of the mentioned year, Radnor took to his Instagram account and posted a couple of pictures.

In one of the images, the actor was seen holding a bouquet of red and white flowers.

His social media post was accompanied by a caption of moderate length.

Radnor stated how sometimes friends who are seemingly in the flower business opt to drop flowers unexpectedly in the middle of a pandemic.

In the later part of the writing, he revealed that the flowers were gifted to him by Spencer.

During the said time, Radnor's currently-rumored girlfriend was prepping to start a flower business called County Line Florals.

Josh Radnor's Relationship Timeline

Before his relationship rumors with Spencer, the HIMYM alum was known to have dated several actresses.

The known details of Radnor's dating history dates back to 2008. At the time, he was reportedly dating Lindsay Price since August.

 Little more than a year later, news of their split was reported by People.

Despite initially meeting on the set of HIMYM in 2007, Radnor and Price's love affair only began eighteen months after they met in New York.

Fast-forward to late 2011, and Radnor was in love again. He was dating Twilight actress and former girlfriend, Jessica Jones.

But, unfortunately, the former couple decided to go their separate ways after over a year of dating.

The actor was then reported dating famous actress Marisa Tomei, known for The Wrestler and the Spider-man movies.

Per Heavy, Radnor and Tomei were spotted together in 2013 at the Bloomberg and Vanity Fair cocktail reception in Washington.

Shortly, after rumors of their relationship started making rounds on the internet.

While it was speculated that they were in love for around a year, neither came forward to either confirm or deny the claims.

And on December 07, 2016, Us Magazine put out a dedicated report and claimed Radnor to be dating actress Minka Kelly.

However, a couple of months later, i.e., March 2, 2017, the news of their short-lived romance was all over the internet.

Interestingly, despite being in many relationships, the actor never married and therefore does not have a wife.
