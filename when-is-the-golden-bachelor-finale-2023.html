Here's When The Golden Bachelor Finale Airs in 2023

'The Golden Bachelor's' first season is shorter than is typical for 'The Bachelor' franchise and fans want to know when the 2023 finale airs on ABC.

The Gist:

  • The Golden Bachelor Season 1 is shorter than seasons of The Bachelor.
  • The two-hour finale of The Golden Bachelor premieres on Thursday, Nov. 30, 2023.
  • Stick around for potential spoilers about Gerry's final pick at the end of this post.

Article continues below advertisement

In many ways, The Golden Bachelor may be the most authentic series in the Bachelor franchise as a whole. Leading man Gerry Turner makes connections from night one and almost every time he has to send someone home, he does so with tears in his eyes. This is a group of much older contestants than viewers are used to and they are all truly here for the right reasons.

Another way The Golden Bachelor differs from the flagship show is its much shorter episodes — at just an hour, they span half the time as The Bachelor and The Bachelorette. And the season itself is shorter than the original series too.

With that said, when is the Season 1 finale of The Golden Bachelor? Keep scrolling to find out!

Plus: If you want to know who Gerry picks during the finale, stick around for Reality Steve's spoilers at the end of this post.

When is 'The Golden Bachelor' finale in 2023?

Since The Golden Bachelor is only eight episodes long, that means the finale is right around the corner — but when, exactly? Well, on Nov. 1, 2023, ABC finally shared the details regarding the rest of the season! So, mark your calendars because the two-hour finale airs on Thursday, Nov. 30, 2023.

If you can't get enough of Gerry and his lovely ladies on Thursday nights, you can re-watch The Golden Bachelor on Hulu the following day. But it's no big surprise for fans since The Bachelor airs on Hulu as well each season.

Will there be a second season of 'The Golden Bachelor'?

As of now, The Golden Bachelor hasn't been renewed for a second season. But the shorter episode length and genuine nature of the castembwrs has fans responding pretty positively to the Bachelor spinoff. So it wouldn't be surprising to get another season of a different Golden Bachelor lead ready to find love again.

For now, though, let's just focus on getting more Golden Bachelor in our lives, OK?
