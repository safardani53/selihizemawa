Who is Gigi Salmon? BBC presenter at Wimbledon 2017 and host of Chelsea TV

SPORTS presenter Gigi Salmon is a BBC regular in the Wimbledon 2017 commentary box.

Here's everything we know about the broadcaster and former host of Chelsea TV...

Who is Gigi Salmon? What's her background?

Gigi Salmon is a TV and radio broadcaster that has been working in the world of sport for more than 20 years.

She attended the private St George's School in Ascot before studying at Oxford Brookes University.

Gigi landed her first job in 1997 when she became the sports presenter then editor on Radio Oxygen in Oxford.

She has then gone on to work for TalkSport, Chelsea TV, the French Tennis Federation and now the BBC.

What will Gigi Salmon be doing during Wimbledon 2017?

Since 2012, Gigi has been a commentator for Live@Wimbledon but this year she is joining the BBC team.

During the tournament, she will be in the commentary with former pro tennis players, giving their analysis on the action at the tournament.

Gigi will be heard on the televised coverage as well as giving commentary on BBC Radio Five Live.

Who else will be joining Gigi Salmon in the commentary box during Wimbledon 2017?

The BBC have a stellar line up commentators and experts ready to give their opinions during the tournament.

As well as Gigi, other broadcasters will include Andrew Cotter, Barry Davies, Nick Mullins and John Inverdale.

They will be joined by former professionals, including Boris Becker, Pat Cash, Andrew Castle, John McEnroe, Tim Henman and Tracy Austin.
