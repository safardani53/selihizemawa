Little People's Matt Roloff SNUBS his son Zach and daughter-in-law Tori in new post about family tri

MATT Roloff has SNUBBED his son Zach and daughter-in-law Tori.

The Little People, Big World star's new post about their family trip threw a little shade.

Matt, 60, and his girlfriend Caryn Chandler are at the Little People of America conference in Washington and they're not alone.

His son Zach and Zach's wife Tori - plus their adorable children Jackson, four, Lilah, two, and Josiah, one month - are there too.

Not that fans would know based on the patriarch's new post.

On July 4th, he shared an Instagram slideshow of three photos, the first some mountains, the second a TLC-tinged reunion and the third a family reunion - of sorts.

He smiled with Our Little Family's Dan Hamill - their show got two seasons a few years back - and 7 Little Johnstons' Trent, which fans loved.

In the third, he grinned while with his ex-wife his Amy Roloff, her new husband Chris Mareck, and Matt's rarer-seen daughter Molly and her husband Joe.

The whiplash-inducing reunion had a strange element, though.

Matt's son Zach and Tori were nowhere to be found in the images, nor were they mentioned.

'STRANGE SNUB'

"Caryn and I are spending a few days at the 2022 National little people conference in Spokane WA…." patriarch Matt began in his caption.

"Lots of highlights seeing Molly and Joel, watching Jackson play soccer with others his age ..

"Hanging out with Chris and Amy… catching up with [7 Little Johnstons'] Trent and Amber and their family." he continued.

"Yes, the little people community is truly a life long family of friends loved and respected and admired … some for my entire life…

"Thanks Caryn for coming to support and be by my side ….

"Next we’re off to AZ for some pool time.. then back to the farm to continue the house build. Busy Summer ahead."

Fans found it odd that Matt mentioned one of Zach's sons, Jackson, but no one else from his family.

One commented, "So nice to see beautiful [daughter] Molly and her husband. Blended family is truly remarkable and refreshing."

Another wrote, "Cool! TLC stars collide."

However, a third tossed some shade back and with a wink emoji, wrote: "So nice to see y’all getting along. Makes life so much easier for your adult children."

Another wrote on Zach's sister and Matt's daughter, "So happy Molly isn’t choosing sides!"

CONFERENCE CLASHING

Over on Tori Roloff's Instagram, it appears their family had lunch with Zach's mom Amy too - a different lunch it seemed.

They also flaunted medals at the conference - it's unclear if Matt and his son Zach's family crossed paths while there offline.

Matt's shade comes as he has feuded with his sons Zach and Jeremy, both 32, about the sale of his famed Oregon farm.

Girlfriend Caryn was at the table for the unsuccessful negotiations with the brothers, which resulted in Matt putting up part of the farm for sale privately. 

Their failure to come to an agreement created a massive rift between Matt and his son Zach, who quit working on the farm and accused the patriarch of not “valuing the next generation”.

Matt's new girlfriend Caryn has also come under fire from fans, who think she’s "overstepping by getting involved."

Matt announced the sale of the Oregon pumpkin farm in May.

According to the Wall Street Journal, Matt has asked for about $4million with the sale of the "original family home and bright red barn."

The "house build" he referenced in his caption would seem to be the ongoing renovations on the farm they can't find a buyer for - after dismissing offers from the two sons.

Zach has expressed his deep disappointment in being "outpriced" by his dad on it.
