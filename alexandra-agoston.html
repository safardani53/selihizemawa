Career

In 2005, she made her New York debut at the Boudicca, Imitation of Christ, Proenza Schouler, and Monique Lhuillier autumn presentations, among others. She was on the cover of the Australian fashion magazine Yen the same year. She walked the Armani Privé and Christian Lacroix couture presentations in Paris in July 2005. During the spring 2006 ready-to-wear season, she took a hiatus from modeling. She returned to the catwalk after a year for the spring Armani Privé, Christian Lacroix, Elie Saab, and Jean-Paul Gaultier show in Paris.

In 2007, she opened the autumn Central Saint Martins student exhibition in London. She also appears in the autumn Christian Dior and John Galliano presentations in Paris, as well as the spring Christian Dior, Christian Lacroix, and Jean-Paul Gaultier couture shows. She was also the face of Jean Paul Gaultier’s Jeans Fall ad campaign. In 2010, she secured a fragrance deal with Moschino Toujours Glamour, which had been hand-picked by Inez van Lamsweerde and Vinoodh Matadin. She has also served as the face of Kookai. She featured in the Spring/Summer 2015 presentation in September 2014, with Jean-Paul Gaultier, John Galliano, and Guy Laroche. She wore Badgley Mischka, Bibhu Mohapatra, Desigual, and Tracy Reese, among other brands.
