Amber Rose on the Kim Kardashian Sex Tape: It's Dope!

Amber Rose would like to clarify negative comments recently made about Khloe Kardashian and her family.

The short-haired model engaged in a heated war of online words with Khloe a few weeks ago, responding to the reality star slamming her past as a stripper by pointing out why Khloe may want to remain quiet on this sort of topic:

Because her family is only famous due to the Kim Kardashian sex tape.

Point, set, match… Amber Rose.

But while Rose has easily won her epic feud with the Kardashians, she tells Entertainment Tonight that it was never really a feud to begin with.

"I don’t hate Khloe. I don’t hate her!” Rose insists. “I can understand why it came across the wrong way to her, I get it."

But Rose, who started this rivalry by slamming Tyga for dating a baby such as Kylie Jenner, simply wishes Khloe had called her to express her disappointment in that comment.

"She didn’t have to tweet 11 times and try to humiliate me," Amber says.

The model did find Khloe’s comments about her stripper past especially hypocritical, however, given the family’s initial rise to fame.

"The only reason these girls have careers is because their older sister had a little fun on tape, let’s be realistic,” Rose said, referencing Kim Kardashian’s 2003 amateur porn with Ray J.

“Which I never, ever hated on that. I’m all for a woman making something out of nothing. If you can do something and bring your entire family on and make millions of dollars, that’s dope to me. I love that."

Rose and Kanye West dated for two years (from 2008 to 2010), and Amber says was deeply hurt by her an interview Kanye gave in which he labeled Rose as dirty.

He said he had to take 30 showers after being with Rose before Kim would sleep with him.

"We were happy when we were together, and now all of the sudden I’m getting slut shamed because we’re not together anymore, and it’s unfair,” she says.

“I feel like women deal with that constantly on a daily basis, and I’m sick of it."
