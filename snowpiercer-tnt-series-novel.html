What book is TNT's Snowpiercer based on?

Le Transperceneige, translating as The Snowpiercer, is a French graphic novel by Jacques Lob.

It was first published in 1982 and there are four volumes in the series: The Escape, The Explorers, The Crossing, and Terminus.

The final volume was published in 2015 - the series has spanned decades.

The stories are set in an imagined world where natural disasters have caused an ice age, while all of humanity are on board a 1,001 carriage train called the Snowpiercer that constantly drives around the world.

At the end of the series a patch of growing flowers is seen and there is hope for life again.

The book also had a film adaption in 2013.
