Rico Verhoeven Net Worth: Earning [2023 Update]

Rico Verhoeven is a Dutch professional kickboxer who competes in the heavyweight division of Glory and has a net worth of $3 million. He is also known as the king of kickboxing. 

Verhoeven is considered one of the best kickboxers in the world. He is the Glory Heavyweight Champion and has retained the title since 2014. 

The Dutch boxer won the vacant title after defeating Romanian professional kickboxer and politician Daniel Florin Ghiță, known as one of the greatest active kickboxers.

Besides that, Verhoeven has fought 70 matches and won 60 while losing 10. Similarly, he has also ventured into the boxing and MMA world. 

The fighter fought one boxing and MMA match in his career and was victorious in both. 

Rico defeated Hesdy Gerges on October 29, 2022, in Rotterdam Ahoy. The fight lasted six rounds, and Verhoeven won the match by technical knockout, recording his sixteenth victory in a row. 

Not to mention, he also has some experience working in films and television. Some of his movies and television programs include Vechtershart, Kickboxer: Retaliation, Hotboxin with Mike Tyson, etc.

Well, we must admit that the kickboxer is an all-rounder and, through his hard work, has achieved the success he has today.

Rico Verhoeven Net Worth | Professional Career

Rico Verhoeven was born on April 10, 1989, in Bergen op Zoom, Netherlands, to his supporting parents, Jos Verhoeven and Jacqueline Deurloo.

The kickboxer’s father was a karate black belt, so he started training his son early on. At age 5, Jos got his son into martial arts; by 7, he was already learning kickboxing. 

Moreover, Rico turned pro as a 15-year-old and has been dominating the kickboxing world since then. He was named the Kickboxer of the Year in 2013 by Bloody Elbow.com. 

Likewise, the fighter was awarded the Glory Heavyweight World Championship Tournament title in 2014. Not to mention, he has defended his Glory Heavyweight Championship title nine times successfully. 

Verhoeven was well known for his rivalry against Daniel Ghiță in his early professional career. He was usually victorious against Ghiță, a well-respected persona in the kickboxing world. 

Furthermore, Kickboxingplanet.com named him the 2016 kickboxer of the year. Similarly, he received the title of Glory Fighter of the Year the following year.

The king of kickboxing won the Glory Knockout of the Year in a match against Jamal Ben Saddik. In 2017, Liverkick.com named him the Fighter of the Year. 

On top of that, he was the Glory Highlight of the Year in 2019. In 2021, Rico was declared the Glory 77 Heavyweight Tournament title winner. 

Rico defeated It’s Showtime World Heavyweight Hesdy Gerges on October 29, 2022, in Rotterdam Ahoy.

The fight lasted six rounds, and Verhoeven won the match by technical knockout, recording his sixteenth victory in a row.
