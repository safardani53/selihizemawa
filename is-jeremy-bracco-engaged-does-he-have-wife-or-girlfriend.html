Is Jeremy Bracco Engaged? Does He Have Wife Or Girlfriend?

Professional ice hockey player Jeremy Bracco has played league games for around eight years. Although he has mostly played outside the United States, ice hockey fans always seem curious about his life, including whether he is engaged to someone.

Bracco is trending on the Internet after a girl on TikTok posted a video claiming that a famous ice hockey player ghosted his girlfriend after eight years of a relationship.

Even though she did not reveal the name, people are guessing it might be Bracco. Did he do that? Does he have a girlfriend? Read this article further to find out!

Jeremy Bracco is an American professional ice hockey player who plays forward for the HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League.

He began his professional career in 2017, playing for the Toronto Marlies in the AHL.

Two years before his professional debut, the Toronto Maple Leafs had chosen him as the 61st overall pick in the 2015 NFL Entry Draft.

After staying three seasons with the Marlies, Bracco went to Europe to continue his professional career. He has already played for several European clubs, including KalPa of the Finnish Liga.

Moreover, he was a member of the US Team at the 2015 IIHF World U18 Championships, where he won the gold.

Is Jeremy Bracco Engaged?

Does He Have A Wife Or Girlfriend?

The young American ice hockey player prefers keeping his personal life away from the limelight, so one cannot say he has a girlfriend with complete certainty. 

He has not posted anything revealing his partner on the Internet so his fans could know. Because of this, it is unclear whether he is engaged, married, or single at the moment.

However, recently, a girl with the username @emmanoyesmaybe posted a video on TikTok saying that one of the renowned ice hockey athletes ghosted her best friend after eight years of a relationship. But she did not reveal who the player was.

Soon after, the video went viral, and people started guessing names and most of them thought it was Bracco. 

In the same video, the girl further added that the athlete posted his girlfriend without tagging her, but they later found out that she had been his girlfriend for a few years.

Similarly, in another picture which was posted a few days later, the athlete’s girlfriend was seen wearing a ring.

While most people already think it is Bracco, one cannot be sure about it without substantial evidence. However, it is pretty surprising that Bracco has kept his Instagram account locked amidst the rumor.

Little On Jeremy Bracco Family

Jeremy Bracco was born to his parents, Michael and Victoria Bracco, on March 17, 1997, in Freeport, New York. 

Born and raised in New York, Jeremy started playing ice hockey when he was four. His dad, Michael, was formerly a goaltender at Dartmouth University and taught his kids how to play the game.

Bracco’s dad served as his coach for nearly six years. Although not much detail about his siblings is known, Jeremy had once said that both his brothers played ice hockey.

Similarly, his uncle, Jon, was a goalie for St. Lawrence University from 1993 to 1997.
