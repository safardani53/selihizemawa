RYAN Seacrest fans are convinced he may still have a host of feelings for his ex-girlfriend of 10 years, Shayna Taylor.

Ryan's girlfriend Aubrey Paige shared Instagram snaps from their ongoing getaway that raised eyebrows given the choice of location.

The American Idol star and Aubrey, 25, enjoyed their ongoing Italian getaway in photos posted by Aubrey on Thursday.

She and Ryan, 48, soaked up the sun during their summer trip, taking an extended vacation there, having been snapped in Portofino at the beginning of July.

In the new post, model Aubrey started with a sweet pic of her and Ryan posing outside a stunning villa.

TV star Ryan looked cool and casual in a navy linen shirt and beige slacks, donning a terrific tan.

MOVING ON

Since 2021, Ryan has been dating New York-based model Aubrey.

Aubrey is repped by Genetics Model Management and is also an aspiring screenwriter.

Ryan and his former Live! co-host Kelly Ripa spoke of the former's relationship with Aubrey on his talk show before his departure from it this year.

Ryan recalled Kelly meeting Aubrey: "Kelly said, 'We're all happy you're here and that you exist because we weren't sure there was somebody.'"

Kelly commented: "We were starting to be like, 'Maybe he doesn't have a special person and maybe we should check in on him more often?' I am so fond of her."

Ryan started dating chef and influencer Shayna Taylor, now 31, in 2013.

The pair were on and off for 10 years until they called it quits in June of 2020.

A Live! with Kelly and Ryan spokesperson said of the former couple: "They remain good friends, each other's biggest supporters, and will always cherish their time together as a couple."

Shayna moved to Italy after the split but now has a boyfriend of her own, according to her Instagram page of 766K followers.

GAME TO TRY OUT?

In June, Wheel of Fortune announced via Instagram that Ryan would take over for Pat Sajak, 76, to host alongside Vanna White, 66.

He will take over after the next season ends and after over 40 years of Pat at the helm.

Along with a photo of Ryan, a caption stated: "It’s official! Starting in 2024, Ryan Seacrest will take the stage as the new host of Wheel of Fortune. 

"We're so excited - Welcome Ryan!"

Amid fan concerns, Ryan confirmed that he would also remain the host of American Idol.

Some fans theorized that Aubrey could join her partner on Wheel of Fortune and take over Vanna's role - as her contract post-2024 is in tense negotiations.

That said, fans also think Pat's daughter Maggie Sajak is in the A-tier to be the next Vanna as she's already involved with the show.
