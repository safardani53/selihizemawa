Kate Middleton in Labor: Celebs Tweet Royal Baby Mania

The moment is finally here! After months of anticipation, Kate Middleton went into labor on Monday, July 22, sending royal baby mania into overdrive on Twitter.

Minutes after the Palace announced in a statement that the pregnant Duchess of Cambridge, 31, had checked into the Lindo Wing at St. Mary's Hospital in London, the microblogging site exploded with #KateMiddleton and #RoyalBaby tweets from all over the world.
