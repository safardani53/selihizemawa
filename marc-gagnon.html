Who is Marc Gagnon:

Marc Gagnon is a famous Speed Skater. He was born on May 24, 1975 and his birthplace is Canada. Marc is also well known as, Speed skater who won three Olympic gold medals, one in the 500 meter race in 2002 and two in the 5,000-meter relay, in 1998 and 2002; inducted into Canada’s Sports Hall of Fame.

Marc is originated from Canada. He and American speed skater Apolo Ohno both took home gold at the 2002 Olympics; Ohno’s gold came in the 1,500 meter race.
