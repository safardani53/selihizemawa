A MONEY-SAVING shopper has revealed some of the bargain buys you can nab at Lidl half-price sale, including must-have items for transforming your garden on a budget.

YouTube star Liyana shared a thorough insight into the incredible sale just in time for summer, walking her subscribers through the local Lidl.

Whilst browsing around the store, the bargain hunter found heaps of affordable buys for gardens and outdoor spaces, such as a 15kg bag of play sand for a mere £3.99.

Right next to it, she also spotted a 40l bag of decorative bark chips retailing for £3.49, making it the perfect addition for a lawn glow-up.

Walking a bit further in the shop, Liyana saw a number of products for repelling insects, for example, a large candle and an electric insect killer (£12.99).

The same buy was also available in a smaller size - selling for £5.99.

With the warmer weather just around the corner, the store also sells a variety of fans, such as a smaller one for your desk priced at £14.99.

As Liyana came to discover, there was everything a foodie would want, including an omelette maker and silicone ice cube moulds for all your refreshing cocktails.
