THE View's Sunny Hostin has gotten real about what she will not wear on the morning show.

On Friday's episode of The View, Sunny decided to forego clear vision in order to look stylish on camera.

During the talk show, Sunny, 54, took a moment before the episode wrapped up to to welcome the members of her home chapter's Alpha Kappa Alpha, Sorority, Incorporated.

The ABC star revealed that the AKAs who sat in the audience were there to support her as she co-hosted The View.

Producers caught Sunny's beautiful sorors dressed in identical green wrap dresses with soft pink trimming and pearl necklaces when they panned the camera to them.

A pearl necklace and green and pink colors are the AKAs calling card.

The TV star called some of her AKA friends who were there by name, well, the ones she was able to see.

"I'm too vain to wear glasses, and so I can't see everyone's faces, but I thank you so much for coming to support me sorors," Sunny said as the group laughed.

While Sunny's vanity might interfere with her vision, it proves useful when it comes to her fashion choices.

Previously, Sunny's co-star Joy Behar, 80, mocked her, taking a brutal NSFW dig at her look for the day.

The former CNN legal analyst's outfit caught the attention of her 80-year-old co-star as soon as the show began.

Initially, attention was brought to Joy's outfit first since she sported a rich fuchsia blazer over a deep pink blouse, giving her a vivacious appearance.

Their co-star Whoopi Goldberg, 67, told Joy that her outfit looked "very sherbert-y" and that she "wanted to lick" her "shoulder" after she observed her look.

Sara Haines, 45, another member of The View panel, chimed in, complimenting Sunny on her equally stylish ensemble so both her and Joy's outfit was spotlighted.

"...Don't forget Sunny's dress, it's beautiful," Sara praised.

Sunny's black skintight dress hugged her petite frame.

It also possessed a sparkly red design on the front of it that had a dash of yellow, which brightened the dress a bit.

'IT'S A FLOWER!'

Sunny tried to downplay the cuteness of her outfit, telling the roundtable that she knew that "the jokes were gonna come," which did, from Joy.

"Sunny has a phallus on her dress," Joy joked, causing everyone to laugh.

Defending her dress, Sunny stood up, pointing to the design on it as she clarified what it was to Joy and the rest of the crew in the studio.

She emphatically protested, "It's a flower!"
