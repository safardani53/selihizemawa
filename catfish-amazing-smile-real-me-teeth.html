MANY of us wish we had the perfect smile, and one woman who regularly gets told she does reveals how everything isn’t quite how it seems.

The internet is full of different types of catfish, whether it’s someone pretending to be someone completely different, or someone who makes themselves look like a whole different person thanks to makeup.

Now TikToker Lilyy Magno, says she gets accused of being a catfish thanks to her smile and fake teeth.

In one video she jokes that people will tell her she has “perfect teeth” to which she then removes her fake teeth, and can sometimes cause quite a stir.

In two different videos she shows the hilarious reactions men in bars have had when she takes out her teeth.

One of the men quickly reacts with, “wooooahh nice!” while the other doesn’t appear to know what’s going for a few seconds before reacting with shock.

One commenter joked that: “He had too many fireballs or something. He needed to warm up a reaction.”

While another quipped: “He was just thinking if he could fill the gap.”

Lilyy has previously spoken out about social media can make people insecure, and that most of what’s posted isn’t real life, including on her own Instagram.

Looking at her own Instagram she says: “Fake, fake, fake - none of this is real, this right here is real [holds up dentures]. Maybe social media is an escape from reality, but think about what it does to your mental health...

"This is why I make videos about my teeth. I'm just trying to be real."

While some may accuse of being a ‘catfish’ Lilyy does have many supporters online, and those that are grateful for what she’s doing in pointing out the fakeness of social media.
