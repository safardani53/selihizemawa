Porsha Williams Gets Into Yet Another Fight

Porsha Williams threw herself a lovely holiday party, which was ruined by the presence of some dude.

Williams had an ax to grind with said dude, who was the plus one of William’s friend, Jami Ziegler.

Ziegler, an image consultant, has appeared on The Real Housewives of Atlanta.

According to TMZ, Williams tried to have Jami thrown out of her party, but Jami was already on her way.

Williams reportedly followed Ziegler out, "shoved her to the ground … then started punching and pulling hair."

Cameras missed the action, and the fight was broken up before the cops arrived.  

The reason for the altercation was reportedly "bad blood" between Williams and Ziegler’ date, so why the two women fought is one of the greatest female mysteries of all time.

This is nothing new for fans; Williams attacked co-star Cynthia Bailey on last night’s episode, and had to be pinned down on her back.

Nearly every fight I’ve seen Williams get into involves her being pinned down by one or more person.  

In the words of a former Housewife, "she is a thug in a cocktail dress."
